Rihanna Topless on Lui Magazine Cover, Reveals Nipple Piercing: Pic

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, April 6, 2024

That's a whole lotta Rihanna! The "What Now" singer, 26, went topless for the cover of the French adult magazine Lui, showing off her breasts and a hard-to-miss nipple piercing. 

The Barbados native is bronzed in the image, showing tan lines around her chest. Her braided hair is hidden under a sun-shielding navy bucket hat, and she is wearing a pair of pink bottoms with a gold body chain. 

The French magazine, which translates to "Him," tweeted the cover photo on Tuesday, April 29, writing, "Rihanna nude on the cover of [issue] seven! Available on newsstands tomorrow." 

Other images from the magazine's photoshoot highlight Rihanna's backside, and once again show her bare breasts. The singer posted the photos to her Instagram account on Tuesday morning, but later deleted the images and inexplicably went on to make her popular account private.
