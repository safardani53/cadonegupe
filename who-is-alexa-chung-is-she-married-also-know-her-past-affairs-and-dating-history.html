Who Is Alexa Chung? Is She Married? Also Know Her Past Affairs And Dating History

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, June 5, 2024

The 33-year old model Alexa Chung is one on the news recently with the launch of her eponymous fashion brand in May 2017. And with her glamorous look and intelligence, she has won millions of hearts. And most of her male fans out there are just crazy for this beautiful model.

Well, to talk about her relationships and affairs, this beautiful lady has been in quite a lot of relationship. And today in this section, this is all we are going to talk about. So let's zoom on.

Alexa Chung and her in-and-out Relationships

Well, let's start from the beginning. Alexa found her first love at the age of 19  in the name of David Titlow, a man twice of her age. David is a fashion photographer. The couple seemed quite serious about each other as they dated each for a 3-long year from 2003 to 2006.

Have to admit, the two look so cute together but all of the good things as it is said, just does not last for long. And on a sad note, the couple broke up after being in a relationship for 3 years.

Following her break up with David, she was rumored to be dating Faris Badwan, the lead singer of the British band . The rumors turned out to be true when the couple publically started dating each other in the same year i.e. 2006. 

Well, this time the relationship didn't seem serious because they broke up within a year. Well, it takes a lot to build up a strong relationship but just a few seconds are enough to pull it to the end. 

But even though they are no longer in a relationship, they are still friends. Well, something is better than nothing, isn't it? 

Here, when the chapter of Badwan ended, it opened the door for a new chapter in Alexa's love life. And the chapter welcomed a new character, Alex Turner, an English musician. 

Alexa started dating Alex, the lead vocalist/guitarist/songwriter of the English band from 2007. The two were THE rock star couple of the late 'noughties'. The couple often spent their time traveling to and from London/New York while they were in a four-year long relationship. 

And this time the model herself admitted that she was really heartbroken after the split.

Even after their split, the couple is in good terms.

The model was also seen wearing t-shirt in around New York in July 2014, almost 3-year after their break up. Well, seems like she still had a feeling for him. What do you guys think about this? 

Well, the deal with the two of them is just known by them, but it's somehow clear that the beautiful model still has got something within her for the musician.

After her relationship with Alex, Alexa was reportedly linked to Theo Hutchcraft in the year of 2011, but their relationship didn't make it to the news. And again in 2015, the model was linked with Alexander Skarsgard, a Swedish actor. Their relationship rumors were all over social sites.

The rumors of their relationship circled when the star and the British model were spotted together for the first time in June 2015. However, both of them later denied the rumors.

Brooklyn Botanical Gardens in Prospect Park, New York

But unfortunately, the couple ended their relationship after two years. Well, may be he didn't make a perfect partner for her! 

Well, it's hard to find true love and until we do, we are way far to treasure the pleasure in our life. This nature of love applies to every being. The same is the case with Alexa and looks like she has not found the perfect one for her. 

So, was it all for Alexa's love life. Or is she currently in a relationship? Let's find out.
