Rebel Wilson Jokes About Workout That Includes Vodka

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, July 14, 2024

She’s on a boat! Rebel Wilson revealed her go-to exercise routine while yachting in Monaco.

“Rebel Fitness onboard Mrs L. #monaco🇮🇩,” the Pitch Perfect actress, 40, captioned her fitness video via Instagram on Sunday, September 27.

The Australia native joked about her new arm workout, while wearing a red and white dress, and holding a large Grey Goose vodka bottle.

“Hey guys, I’d just like to show you my new workout routine,” she began her tutorial. “I call this the Monaco routine.”

The Cats actress then hoisted the vodka bottle up and down as she showed off the arm exercises.

“Basically, we’re just going to work the biceps,” she explained, noting the bottle is 4.5 liters (or roughly 1.2 gallons) of alcohol. “I’m really trying to tone the upper arms. So, we first do some biceps and then take it over head, slight bend in the knees. You really want to feel that burn.”

After demonstrating how to lift the improvised weight for maximum affect, Wilson started running to show how one can add legs into the vodka-based routine. “You get the picture,” she said after breaking into a quick run. “And then that’s basically your arm workout for the day.”

The same day, the Jojo Rabbit star and her boyfriend, Jacob Busch, shared photos together from their trip to Monaco.

“Meeting the Prince with a Prince Charming by my side – what a night to remember in Monaco 🇲🇨,” Wilson captioned a series of photos on Sunday.

The couple stepped out in Monaco over the weekend to attend the opening of the Miró exhibition at the Nouveau Musée National Monaco. There they met Prince Albert II and Isabelle Bscher, the art exhibit curator.

“Incredible evening supporting Prince Albert II, and many others, for the exhibition for Miro curated by @busyisi and @gmurzynska,” Busch wrote via Instagram alongside his own group of photos from the event.

Two days prior, Us Weekly confirmed that the couple have been dating since before the coronavirus pandemic hit earlier this year.

“They are officially boyfriend and girlfriend,” a source exclusively told Us on Friday, September 25.

The pair met in 2019 and were “casually dating” before the health crisis began. “They kept in touch when Rebel was in Australia and when she returned back to the U.S., they picked up dating again and became serious,” the insider added.

The Hustle actress and the Napp’s dairy free ice cream founder made their red carpet debut on Thursday, September 24. The same day, the pair became Instagram official ahead of Prince Albert’s Planetary Health Gala.

“Jacob adored Rebel last year before she went on her health journey,” the source told Us, referring to Wilson’s recent weight loss. “He seems to be very in love with her.”
