Hailey Baldwin Strongly Implies She's Pregnant with Justin Bieber's Baby

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, March 16, 2024

Justin Bieber clearly has a lot on his mind these days.

Did you read the singer’s extremely candid Instragram post about his past drug use and struggles with fame from just a short while ago?

In segue news, meanwhile, Bieber’s wife just hinted online that she may or may not have a lot on her mind — but she very well could have something in her womb.

By which we mean the following:

Baldwin is pregnant with Bieber’s baby!

We think!

The model didn’t come straight out and confirm this amazing news, yet she wrote a comment on Instagram page that almost can’t be interpreted in any other way.

Hours after the 25-year old artist shared his aforementioned emotional message on social media about his anxiety and general mental state, Baldwin posted a cryptic message of her own.

“U r very fine,” she wrote on a photo of Bieber standing beside a bonfire during their recent romantic beach retreat. “Hi (future) baby Daddy."

Like we just said: How else can one interpret this reference?!?

It’s possible that Baldwin isn’t growing a child inside her womb right now.

She may just be talking about her upcoming plans to start a family with the singer, especially because Hailey has made it apparent she wants to have lots of Bieber babies.

"I love kids," Hailey said this past November. "I can’t wait to have my own."

Bieber has also made remarks about how excited he is to be a father, which is why a pregnancy announcement at this juncture wouldn’t really come as a shock.

The stars, remember, are actually married, having entered a New York City courthouse last September and completed the necessary paperwork.

Look for them to throw it down with a lavish wedding at some point in the very near future.

Toward the end of the revealing Instagram post shared by Bieber this week, the singer writes that his marriage to Baldwin has really set him straight.

It has made him focus on what’s truly important in life.

"It’s taken me years to bounce back from all of these terrible decisions, fix broken relationships, and change relationship habits. Luckily god blessed me with extraordinary people who love me for me," he wrote, adding of his relationship with Baldwin:

"Now i am navigating the best season of my life ‘MARRIAGE’ !! Which is an amazing crazy new responsibility,"

What makes marriage so incredible?

Explained Bieber:

"You learn patience, trust, commitment, kindness, humility and all of the things it looks like to be a good man."

Sounds like the sort of lessons a father would want to pass along to his son or daughter?

If Baldwin really is pregnant, allow The Hollywood Gossip to be among the first outlets to say the following: CONGRATULATIONS!
