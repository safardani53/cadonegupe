Jacqie Campos Biography

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, February 25, 2024

Jacqie Campos is an American actress and reality star who has achieved fame and success through her determination and perseverance. She is the second daughter of Regional Mexican singer Jenni Rivera, who was widely considered one of the most influential women in Mexican music.

Jacqie has had a complicated relationship with her father, who was found guilty of pederasty and evasion of justice in 2007 and sentenced to 31 years in prison. In 2017, she expressed forgiveness for what he had done, attributing it to her faith and her desire to move on from the tragedy.

Early Life and Background of Jacqie Campos

Born as Jacquelin Melina Marin on November 20, 1989, in Long Beach, California, Jacqie Campos was destined to be part of the entertainment world. She is one of the daughters of the late Jenni Rivera, an iconic singer, songwriter, and actress. Jenni was celebrated as one of the top-selling female artists in Regional Mexican music, garnering accolades from prestigious media outlets like CNN and Fox News.

Jacqie Campos endured numerous challenges during her formative years. Tragically, her mother Jenni Rivera lost her life in a plane crash near Monterrey, Mexico, on December 9, 2012, leaving Jacqie and her siblings devastated by the sudden loss.

Jacqie and her sister Chiquis Rivera faced a harrowing experience, as they were molested by their father during their childhood. The incident remained hidden for several years and only came to light after their parents' divorce. In 2006, Jacqie's father, Jose Trinidad Marin, was arrested and later sentenced to 31 years in prison after being found guilty.

Jacqie and Michael Campos are blessed with four beautiful children, namely Jordan Faith, Julian Joy, and Jenavieve Campos. Additionally, Jacqie has a daughter named Jaylah Hope Yanez from a previous relationship with Danny Yanez, with whom she was romantically involved from 2008 until 2011.
