Is Kindly Myers Currently Dating ? Know about her Affairs and Relationship Rumors

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, June 12, 2024

When we see superhot models staying away from dating and relationships, we barely believe it at first glance. A bikini model like Kindly Myers is the dream girl of many men who have a secret crush on her. And why not? Considering her sizzling beauty and perfectly toned body, we can barely find a reason why men are not attracted.

Moreover, the fact that Myers doesn’t post any photos with men gives her fans something to dream about. Myers does not talk about her affairs and dating life as she does about her profession. The Tennessee girl, who moved to Nashville to pursue her career, is mysterious when it comes to her private life. So, is she single or dating some secretly? We shall reveal it soon. Keep reading:

Kindly Myers' background and secret love life

Myers is a Kentucky-born girl who was raised by her mother along with her two siblings in Tennessee after her father passed away. In the early days, the gorgeous diva was very much into basketball and horses.

 

She joined the army after her high school graduation and worked there for 4 years as an Automated Logistical Specialist. Later, she moved to Nashville where she finally started her career as a  professional model.

 Myers has so far not been spotted going out with any man whom we can designate as her boyfriend. In fact, her past records of love and relationships are pretty clean. Now, if you have started to doubt her sexual preference, then you should know that Myers isn't gay at all!

Kindly Myers and her boyfriend Harrison Yonts

Myers has been seeing Harrison Yonts for the past few years. Secretly. Well, that’s true! Although Myers has been successful in keeping her rather clean, she once tweeted something on her official handle that made us catch her red-handed. She wrote,“On a date with my love @hyonts at our favorite Nashville spot #mikado”
