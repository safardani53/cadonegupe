Nathan Harrell East is an American singer of jazz, R&B and bass rock. With over 2,000 records, East is considered one of the most recorded bassists in the history of music. East holds a Bachelor of Music degree from the University of California at San Diego. He is a founding member of the contemporary jazz quartet Fourplay and has recorded, performed and co-composed songs with performers such as Bobby Womack, Eric Clapton, Michael Jackson, Joe Satriani, Peter Gabriel, George Harrison, Ringo Starr, Phil Collins, Stevie Wonder, Toto, Kenny Loggins, Daft Punk, Chick Corea and Herbie Hancock.
