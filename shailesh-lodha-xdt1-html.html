TV Actor Shailesh Lodha was born on November 8, 1969 in Jodhpur, India (He's 54 years old now).

Actor, comedian and writer who famously played the role of Taarak Mehta on the sitcom Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chasmah, which holds the Guiness World Record for longest-running Indian daily sitcom. He was also the presenter for Wah! Wah! Kya Baat Hai! and performed in the comedy film Wig Boss. As an author, his works include several satirical/humorous books, a self-help book, and a poetry collection called Dilijale Ka Facebook Status.
