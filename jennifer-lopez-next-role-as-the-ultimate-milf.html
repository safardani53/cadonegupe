Jennifer Lopez: The Ultimate MILF?!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, October 1, 2024

Whether you’re admiring Jennifer Lopez in no makeup or ogling that Versace dress that inspired Google images, there’s no doubt she is a bona fide beauty.

Now it looks like the hot mama is cementing her status as a MILF. Lopez, 45, has reportedly signed on to her next film, a comedy titled Mothers I’d Like To…

Yes, that is a real movie.

According to Deadline, there’s no official logline yet for Mothers I’d Like To...

However, they do note the film is "Bridesmaids meets First Wives Club."

Lopez will produce the film along with her producing partner Elaine Goldsmith-Thomas and Benny Medina.

The script will be written by Mark Perez, who’s credits include Accepted, The Country Bears, and the upcoming Game Night. 

Lopez, who is mom to seven-year-old twins Emme and Max, recently starred in The Boy Next Door where her character hooked up with a high school senior before he turned into a psycho stalker. 

Perhaps Lopez will popularize a new genre of film: Cougar Cinema!
