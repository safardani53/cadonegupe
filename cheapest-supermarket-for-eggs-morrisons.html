I'm a savvy shopper, you need to run to Morrisons for an essential that's 78p you wont find i

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, March 4, 2024

A SAVVY shopper has revealed that fellow bargain hunters should be forming an orderly queue at Morrisons.

That’s because the popular supermarket is selling a kitchen essential for just 78p – considerably cheaper than their rivals.

Social media user and shopping influencer Cost of Living Crisis Tips took to their profile to spread the word on the food must-buy.

He explained via his account @costoflivingcrisistips: “Morrisons are selling six free-range eggs for just 78p.

“With the price of eggs these days, this is a bargain.”

Cost of Living Crisis Tips then zoomed in on the packet so that Morrisons customers could keep their eyes peeled.

He showed how they came in a white box with ‘SAVERS’ printed on the top next to part of a blue circle. 

But he said there was a small catch, as there’s a particular place you’ll spot them – and they’re not always easy to find.

He continued to his 245k followers: “They are usually on the bottom shelf, so you’ll have to look out for them.”

As soon as he posted the advice, he was inundated with comments from shoppers saying other supermarket stores were selling eggs for as little as 20 for £1.50.

But, as the man in the know pointed out, these would not be free range, as another person added: “Free-range should be the only option.” 

One more social media user added: “Thanks for this – I’m paying £2 odd for a dozen at Aldi. Thanks again.”

A third grateful shopper wrote: “Love this – you have shown me some right bargains. Keep up the advice mate.”

As a fourth typed: “Cheaper than Aldi.”

Others were also keen to thank him for his advice, as they said they’d be heading to Morrisons as soon as they could.

One TikToker wrote with their tongue firmly in their cheek: “I’m shell shocked, that’s a cracking deal.”

While another added: “Bargain lad!”

Others also said that his tip to look at the bottom of the shelf was genius.

One person pointed out: “Eye level is a rip off!”
