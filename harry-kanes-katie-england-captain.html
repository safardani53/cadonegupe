Harry Kane's wife Katie shows off beach body in animal print swimsuit leaving loved-up England capta

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, October 6, 2024

HARRY KANE showed that romance with wife Katie is alive and kicking after being left in awe with her holiday snap.

The loved-up pair jetted to Portugal to soak up the sun before the England captain returned for his international duties.

The couple were eager to show their followers that they were enjoying a rare break by posting pictures lounging by the pool.

And Katie flaunted her figure on the terrace while wearing a leopard-print swimsuit.

She uploaded an image of her toned physique with the caption: "Beach, please."

Kane, who married Katie in 2019, commented 'my wife' alongside a fire emoji.

Kane also showed off his pride and joys on their trip... after posting a picture of the Golden Boot and Playmaker award next to the pool.

Alongside the image he wrote the caption: "Took the little ones on their first holiday."

England boss Gareth Southgate will be hoping Kane, who played 46 times in all competitions last season, will come back from his holiday refreshed and ready to fire on all cylinders.

The Three Lions skipper was among the England squad who joined up for training ahead of the Euros tournament on Sunday.

Kane's future will be the hot topic of conversation over the tournament after letting it be known he is considering leaving Tottenham in search of silverware.

Manchester City are thought to be leading the pack and SunSport revealed how Raheem Sterling and Gabriel Jesus could be offered as a makeweight in the deal.

But Southgate is confident his summer of uncertainty will have no impact on his form over the Euros.

He said: "I don't ever need to have a conversation with Harry Kane regarding focus or professionalism.

"He has one goal this summer while he's with us and that's to win a European Championship.
