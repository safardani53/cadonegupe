Glossier launches Limited Edition Cookie Butter Balm Dotcom and we can't wait to get our hands on it

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, May 30, 2024

GLOSSIER has launched a limited edition flavour of their cult favourite lip balm, Cookie Butter Balm Dotcom.

Inspired by fresh out of the oven cookies and the tastes, scents and cosiness of changing seasons.

The new limited edition Balm Dot Com is perfect for Autumn, and features the nourishing, hydrating formula that Glossier fans love but with a twist.

  • Cookie Butter Balm Dotcom, £10, Glossier - buy here

Cookie Butter Balm Dotcom features a sheer melted caramel tint leaving a barely-there warm sheen on the lips, with "a touch of cinammon-y spiced butter biscuit taste".

It's available from glossier.com and is priced at £10 - we can't wait to get our hands on ours.

The Cookie Butter Balm Dotcom follows in the footsteps of the last limited edition Fig Balm Dotcom and joins the ranks of "Original (which doubles as a skin salve), and other flavoured formulas Mint, Rose, Coconut, Birthday, Cherry, Mango, and Berry."

With key ingredients including Castor Seed Oil and Besswax to hold on to moisture, Cupuacu Fruit Extract with Omega-6 and Omega-9 fatty acids that melt into skin on contact, and Rosemary Leaf Extract which is rich in botanical antioxidants.

Beauty fans can also pick up the Balm Dotcom Trio, which is a customisable three pack of Glossier's cult favorite universal skin salve - and you can choose your own flavours.

You can shop Balm Dotcom Cookie Butter from glossier.com from £10.
