KIM Kardashian has revealed her stacked address book of A-list celebrities that she planned to text for their help in her law efforts.

The Kardashians star made a couple of shocking confessions during last night's episode.

In one scene, Kim, 41, sat with film producer Scott Budnick as they discussed his Anti-Recidivism Coalition for social justice.

The reality star, who has decided to fight for the rights of the wrongly incarcerated, worked with the Hangover creator on the Julius Jones case.

While discussing their options, Kim said she would personally text some of her celebrity friends to get them to speak out about Julius.

She created a list of her contacts that could potentially help reach out to the governor about the case.

Amongst the celebrities on the whiteboard were: Jake Gyllenhaal, Demi Lovato, Katy Perry, Blake Shelton, Gwyneth Paltrow, Amy Schumer, James Corden, Ashley Graham, Van Jones, and Dr. Oz.

Kim then said with a laugh: “Honestly, I’m sure if I call any athlete, if I wanted a call with them, they would pick up because they would think that I wanted to hook up with them.” 

During Wednesday's episode, the TV personality dropped several bombs about her life.

BOTOX BOMB

The mother of four revealed that she still gets Botox despite previously vowing to never have it again.

The reality star first had the injections when she was 29 back in 2010 but swore off the procedure after her skin had a nasty reaction to it.

But in the latest episode of The Kardashians, the 41-year-old let it slip that she still gets them.

The SKIMS founder was chatting with Scott Budnick about how her late dad Robert demanded she didn’t become an attorney because she was “so vain”.

Kim said: “He would be proud. He would get a kick out of it because he used to tell me ‘you don’t want to do this.’

"He would tell me ‘you’re so vain, you are not going to want to be an attorney.

"And you’ll have so many f**king wrinkles from the stress of this job. I know you, you won’t want it.’”

Scott smiled wryly and said: "Look how much you’ve changed."

Kim smiled back and said: “I know. And thank God for Botox.” 

PLASTIC SURGERY PROBLEMS

Back in season five of Keeping Up With The Kardashians, Kim got Botox around her eyes several weeks before her 30th birthday.

She reacted badly to the injections, suffering severe bruising around her eyes as well as itching and burning.

The TV star swore off the procedure, telling producers in a confessional: “I will never do this again," before adding that she wouldn’t do it again “before 40".

Kim has faced years of gossip over whether she's had a nose job and fillers, as well as speculation over her famous curves.
