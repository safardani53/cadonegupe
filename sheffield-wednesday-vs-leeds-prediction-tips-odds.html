Sheffield Wednesday vs Leeds United: Prediction, tips and odds

LEEDS UNITED will hope to get back to winning ways when they travel to Sheffield Wednesday.

Marcelo Bielsa's men suffered their first league defeat of the season in a shock result against Birmingham City last weekend.

  • DIAMOND: Over 2.5 goals to be scored, 9/10 with Unibet
  • NEXT BEST: A red card to be shown in the match, 4/1 with Betfair
  • LONGSHOT: Fernando Forestieri and Jack Harrison both to score anytime, 14/1 with Unibet

Wednesday are yet to keep a clean-sheet this season and Leeds are the Championship's second-highest goalscorers.

Goals aplenty are expected in this one and you can back over 2.5 goals to be scored at an enticing 9/10 with Unibet.

The encounter is likely to be a highly-charged Yorkshire derby that will see the players leave everything out on the pitch.

It could boil over between the sides and the referee might well have a busy day on his hands.
