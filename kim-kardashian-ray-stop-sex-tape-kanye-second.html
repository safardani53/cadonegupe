Kim Kardashian's ex Ray J begs fans to 'stop' talking about sex tape as Kanye West claims there's a

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, April 29, 2024

KIM Kardashian's ex Ray J has begged fans to stop talking about their 2007 sex tape for the sake of his children.

Kanye West drew attention to the leaked graphic video this week when he claimed there is a second sex tape.

The rapper, 44, claimed on Hollywood Unlocked that he flew to meet Ray J and obtain the laptop which contained the alleged second tape.

Kim later slammed Kanye's claims and Ray, 41, has now also shut down the speculation.

Without directly addressing the tape, he tweeted on Wednesday: "This needs to stop. I also have kids."

The singer has two children with ex-wife Princess Love - daughter Melody, three, and son Epik, two.

Kanye claimed earlier this week that he took Kim and Ray's "second sex tape" and returned it to the SKIMS founder.

"I went and got the laptop from Ray J myself that night," he said.

"I met this man at the airport, then got on a red-eye, came back [and] delivered it to her at 8am in the morning."

Kanye said: "She [Kim] cried when she saw it.

"You know why she cried when she seen it on the laptop? Because it represents how much she's been used.

"It represents how much people didn't love her, and they saw her as a commodity."

Kim shut down Kanye's claims, with her rep telling Page Six: "The computer and hard drive collected were supposed to contain the original video and any unseen footage.

KIM'S DENIAL

"After review, there was nothing sexual unseen, only footage on the plane on the way to Mexico and footage at a club and restaurant on the same trip."

The statement continued: "Kim remains firm in her belief that there is no new second tape that exists.

"After 20 years, she truly wishes to move on from this chapter [and] focus instead on the positive things she continues to do as a mother, entrepreneur and advocate for justice reform."

The initial sex tape leaked in 2007, not long before the premiere of Keeping Up With the Kardashians.

Rumors have been swirling about a second part to the sex tape for some time.

SECOND TAPE RUMORS

In September 2021, Ray's ex manager, Wack 100, appeared on a podcast where he claimed he had "part two" of the sex tape which was "better and more graphic".

Kim's attorney, Marty Singer, quickly shut those claims down.

He said at the time in a statement to Page Six: "The claim that there is an unreleased sex tape is unequivocally false.

"It's unfortunate that people make these statements to try and get their 15 minutes of fame."

Ray J also addressed the claim, saying rumors that there's another part to the tape "ain't cool."

He wrote on social media: "This ain't cool - I been staying off the Raydar - Just staying focused and being humble and thankful for all the blessings I'm receiving.
