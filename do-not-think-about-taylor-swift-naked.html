Stop! Do Not Think About Taylor Swift Naked!

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

That’s not a request, it’s a direct order from the cute blonde herself: Taylor Swift really does not want you sitting there, thinking about her naked.

Seriously, she already said this to Rolling Stone. Now she’s reiterating her point to Allure – she’s fine talking dating, but no questions about sex.

“It’s fine to talk about love publicly,” the 19-year-old singer says, “but I think when you talk about virginity and sex publicly, people just automatically picture you naked. So as much as I can prevent people picturing me naked, I’m going to.”

Still, Taylor Swift thinks a lot about dating, especially its impact on her songwriting. Even a bad relationship can be a point of inspiration.

“Let’s say I date a guy who does nothing but damage me,” she says. “You’ve spent time, spent effort, put everything into trying to make that work, and it didn’t. So you write a song about it. It was worth everything if you write a song about it.”

Many of Swift’s songs have romantic overtones, and she lists fairy tales as an inspiration: “I have always been fascinated with fairy tales, and the idea that Prince Charming is just one castle away,” the country star said.

Despite her love of a happy ending, the singer says the flip side can be more appealing: “For some reason, I could never stop writing songs about heartbreak. Just because as human beings, what we can’t have is what we replay in our head.”
