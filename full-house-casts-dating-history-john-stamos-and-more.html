Full House Casts Dating History: John Stamos and More

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, August 11, 2024

The cast of Full House became a tight-knit family after starring on the sitcom for eight seasons from 1987 to 1995.

The series followed Danny Tanner (played by the late Bob Saget) and his three daughters — D.J. (Candace Cameron Bure), Stephanie (Jodie Sweetin) and Michelle (Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen) — as they tried to navigate their new normal after his wife’s passing.

Danny also had the help of his brother-in-law, Jesse Katsopolis (John Stamos), pal Joey Gladstone (Dave Coulier) and his Wake Up San Francisco cohost Rebecca Donaldson (Lori Loughlin).

While most of the stars reunited for the five-season Netflix revival, Fuller House, in 2016, their bond went beyond being a TV family. (Mary-Kate and Ashley did not appear in the revival.)
