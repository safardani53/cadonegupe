Nick Carters Photos With Siblings Aaron, Angel, Leslie and Bobbie Jean Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, March 19, 2024

The Carter family lost their siblings Leslie, Aaron and Bobbie Jean over the years. Look back at their memories together.

The Carter family has suffered several losses over the years. In 2012, they lost their eldest sister, Leslie Carter, when she was just 25 years old. Ten years later, Aaron Carter was found dead in his home at the age of 34, and a year later, Bobbie Jean Carter had died. The surviving Carter siblings are Nick Carter and Aaron’s twin, Angel Carter in addition to Virginia Marie, Kaden Brent and step-sibling Taelyn Dobson.

The brothers and sisters all appeared on their own reality TV series, House of Carters. During those years, they appeared at multiple red carpet events and premieres together. Nick’s singing career with the Backstreet Boys initially took center stage for most of their lives, but Aaron built his own rap and pop career, and Angel became an outspoken advocate about mental health following her twin brother’s death.
