Mel C performs with bearded Ginger Spice drag queen at Brighton Pride

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, May 14, 2024

MEL C performed with a bearded Ginger Spice drag queen at Brighton Pride last night.

The 45-year-old Spice Girl had an almost-familiar face alongside her as she sang at the Pavillon Gardens Brighton with Sink the Pink.

Mel wowed revellers when she took to the stage at midnight as part of the Pride event.

The star performed a mixture of her solo hits, Spice Girls classics, and covers with the Sink the Pink troupe at the packed-out venue.

Mel looked sensational in a PVC top and tracksuit bottoms, wearing a transgender pride/ binary neutral flag.

She had her hair tied up into her iconic Sporty Spice ponytail and is said to have got emotional at times as she spoke to the crowd.

Meanwhile, a drag artist donned a Union Jack corset in homage to Geri Horner's famous Brit Awards dress from 1997.

They teamed the patriotic gear with black pants and a Ginger Spice wig - complete with the two blonde strips at the front, a stark contrast to the otherwise-red locks.

Speaking previously about the performance, Mel shared: "This is a bit of dream come true for me.

"I’m going on tour with Sink the Pink, the most inclusive, fun and fabulous club night in London and we’re taking their best drag queens round the world, for a truly special show. I can’t wait."

Mel's appearance at the event comes just weeks after the Spice Girls concluded their UK reunion tour.

Mel teamed up with her former bandmates - including Geri - for a series of sold-out gigs.
