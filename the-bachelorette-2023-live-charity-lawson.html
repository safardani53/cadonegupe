Fans are convinced Charity and winner 'spoiled' ending

Charity, 27, recently shared a photo on her Instagram Story that showed two drinks and a candle on top of a table in a dimly lit room.

The candle was from the restaurant Dutch Fred's, located in New York City, as Charity was in the city doing multiple rounds of press.

Roughly 24 hours later, one of her current contestants, Dotun Olubeko, shared a similar IG Story of a few appetizers he had started digging into at a restaurant, seemingly while sitting across from someone else.

He included the geotag of Dutch Fred's on the snap.

Reality Steve reshared Dotun's photo on Twitter, as the contestant "quickly deleted" it from his IG. He later said on his Daily Roundup podcast, saying it can be assumed the two were together and Charity may have spoiled the ending to her season.
