Bernie Taupins Words of Praise for Wife Sums up the Bond They Share Together

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 2, 2024

Commemorating twenty-two years of togetherness, star lyricist Bernie Taupin posted a couple of pictures of him and Heather together with captions full of praises about her. 

On August 22, 2020, Bernie added to his Instagram handle, a collection of his wife's pictures. As if posting those lovely pictures was not enough, the star lyricist went on to add a romantic caption for his gorgeous spouse.

The lyricist celebrated the fact that he was dealt a winning hand on that day twenty-two years ago, and he still clung to the very hand. Further, highlighting his love for Heather, Bernie addressed her as his soul, inspiration, and best friend. 

Following this romantic gesture, on December 31, 2020, Bernie made another post dedicated to his spouse on her birthday. In this one, there were some more pictures of the couple with their two daughters.

In the caption, the father of two complimented Heather for being not only a great wife but also a great mother. Mentioning his wife as the love of his life, the lyricist chipped in some extra compliments for his wife for being patient, pure, and timelessly beautiful.

On December 29, 2020, Bernie made a birthday post for his youngest daughter Georgey on his Instagram. In the picture, the father-daughter duo is in the comfort of their home, with Bernie embracing his daughter in his arms. 

Furthermore, in the caption, the lyricist wished his daughter on her 13th birthday and mentioned that he was proud of her and everything that she is.

His elder daughter Charley has made fewer appearances in the star's posts in comparison to Georgey. 

However, on May 18, 2020, Bernie posted a picture of Charley on her birthday.

In the picture, Charley was posing with a brown horse. She was wearing a beige off-shoulder t-shirt with small colorful flower prints paired with a pair of blue jeans. 

As for the caption, Bernie wished his daughter a happy 15th birthday and let her know how unbelievably proud he was to be her father.
