Kate Moss spotted smoking - after claiming she'd given it up and switched to vapes

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, July 22, 2024

SUPERMODEL Kate Moss has been pictured smoking just weeks after seemingly trading tobacco for vaping.

The former party girl, 49, puffed away outside swanky Mayfair restaurant Cecconi's as she caught up with her brother Nick and dad Peter.

Icon Kate went makeup-free for the low-key meet up and wore a floaty pink shirt, ankle-length trousers and heels.

The blonde beauty looked happy and carefree as she chatted outside in the warm sunshine.

Tobacco-loving Kate has been smoking for 34 years and has been snapped many times over the years with a fag in hand.

But last month it appeared that Kate had traded her favoured tobacco for a £4.99 Bloody Mary e-cigarette.

The model was photographed walking in West London with her daughter Lila, 20, as they shared the budget vape.

While the e-cig was low-cost, there was nothing cheap about the pair's outfits and accessories. Lila's Prada handbag alone cost staggering £2,585.

In her catwalk pomp, Kate was almost as famous for her wild partying as she was for strutting her stuff in designer clothing.

As a member of the infamous Primrose Hill set, Kate was often pictured looking bleary-eyed after late hedonistic nights in the capital.

In 2020, Kate's DJ pal Fat Tony, 56, revealed how Kate had turned her back on drink and drugs.

He said: "Kate's been clean for over two years. Me and my sober mates now have a better time than we ever did when we used to drink and take drugs."

Kate now has her own high-end wellness brand Cosmoss and enjoys yoga.

She said of her lifestyle change last year: "When I started to take care of myself things began to change. I think I'm the happiest I've ever been."
