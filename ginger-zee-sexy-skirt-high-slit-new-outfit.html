Ginger Zee shows major skin in sexy leather skirt with high slit for leggy morning show look

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, April 16, 2024

GMA's Ginger Zee has shown off her long legs in a sexy leather skirt with a high slit.

She was trying on a new piece of clothing and wanted to show off the outfit she paired it with.

Ginger has been on a "no new clothes" journey for some time.

That means she has not been buying any new outfits to add to her closet.

Instead, she's been renting from a service called Rent the Runway.

She took to her personal Instagram to talk about what she's been doing with the rental service and posted a photo she wore on TV recently.

Her newest outfit consisted of a long-sleeved paisley purple top and figure-hugging beige skirt.

The shiny leather skirt featured a slit on Ginger's left leg that crept all the way up her thigh, showing off her shapely legs.

Ginger captioned her photo: "Along this #nonewclothes journey, renting has been key to spice up my existing wardrobe for tv! Today is rented top & skirt ( @renttherunway & @wanderlux_style ) - there are so many rental options popping up like @armoire.style too. Not an ad for any of these and depending on how far and how often, renting isn’t ALWAYS the most environmentally friendly— but it sure beats new. If you can find one that services near you (both that I’m using have warehouses within 50 miles of me) thats outstanding. #rent #SustainableClothes #Sustainability #Rental #RentTheRunway #Luxury #Cheaper #LessEmissions #LessWaterUse #Fashion #BehindTheScenes #BTS"

CAN'T UNZEE IT

Recently, Ginger took an unusual seat for her latest video as she discussed a topic that is a big debate in her house.

The Good Morning America meteorologist sat on the toilet in the bizarre video she shared to TikTok on Monday.

The video started off with the bathroom door opening to reveal Ginger, 42, perched on her toilet.

"You know what a huge topic of conversation in my house is?" she asked.

"Toilet paper."

Ginger went on to say that when her children went to their grandmom's house that the toilet paper was "so soft in Arizona."

She stood by her bathroom sink now to explain.

"And that's because I have made us have toilet paper that is good for the planet," she explained.

"Cause that's the thing. We are obsessed with toilet paper in our country."

Ginger went on: "Per capita, 141 rolls annually, the most by any nation."

ENVIRONMENTALLY FRIENDLY

"The NRDC, which is a non-profit that tracks this kind of thing, says that at least a million acres of Canadian boreal forest, so environmentally important, are clear cut, in part, to make this stuff," she explained.

"Now, if you get 100 percent recycled post-consumer waste toilet paper, then you've done your best."

Ginger said that getting that toilet paper is a little sacrifice that can make a huge difference.

"You'll be fine. It still does the job," she ended.

Ginger then shared that the best thing to do to save the planet is to buy a bidet.

She then whispered: "That's my next step. Don't tell Ben."

Ben Aaron is Ginger's husband, whom she married in 2014.
