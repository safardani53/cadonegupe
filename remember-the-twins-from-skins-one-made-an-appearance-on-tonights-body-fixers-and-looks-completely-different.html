Megan Prescott from Skins looks completely different on Body Fixers

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, June 13, 2024

VIEWERS of Body Fixers were given a blast from the past on tonight's episode when a former cast member of Skins showed up on the beauticians sofa.

Megan Prescott, 25, first came onto our screens alongside twin sister Kathryn as Emily and Katie Fitch on smash-hit show Skins series three and four.

But on today's show she gave fans at home a shock when she revealed she had traded in her acting carer and was on the show to get a mahogony tan ahead of her latest competition as a BODY BUILDER.

Flexing her muscles to the cameras and staff members, Meg has clearly been putting her time at the gym and now has a killer eight-pack and defined muscles in her legs and arms.

The tan she requested on the show was so dark in order to show off her muscles, the beautician had to use ROLLERS in order to apply it.

While she appears miles away from the dainty figure she cut when she was on the E4 show back in 2010, Meg hasn't changed at all facially and left her immediately recognisable to those in front of the cameras and watching at home.

The former child star was more than happy to discuss her time on the show while she was tanned up, and said that while her sister Kathryn was supportive in her new venture, she wouldn't be joining her twin in any upcoming competitions.

Megan said: “I don’t think it’s something she’s ever had much interest in.”

On the show, the teenagers were famed for taking drugs and drinking heavily as the teen drama unfolded.

However she admitted that behind the cameras, due to their age, it was a very different story and the group were generally a lot more well behaved than their counterparts.

She said: "A lot of them actually went to the same college as me and we were doing exams. So that was fun."

When her transformation was revealed, Megan's pale white skin was completely transformed into a deep bronze, shocking her as well as other members of the Body Fixers team.

Megan revealed that while this wasn't something she would have permanently, it was necessary to flaunt her hard-earned muscles on stage in competitions.

She was then guided out of the room for fear that her tan would rub off on those around her.

Viewers of the show were more than taken with her new look, and flooded Twitter with praise.

One wrote: "Can't believe how different Meg looks from Skins ! Suits her"

Another added: "@Meg_Prescott is giving me serious body envy, wow"

A third responded: "I'd actually love to be that tanned all the time! #bodyfixers"

This episode also featured a man who had his pubic hair shaved and dyed into a pink crown - complete with a 'pejazzle' outline.

Body Fixers continues next Tuesday at 9pm on E4.
