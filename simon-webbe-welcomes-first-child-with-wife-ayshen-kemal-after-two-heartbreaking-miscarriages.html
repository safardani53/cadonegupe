Simon Webbe Welcomes First Child With Wife Ayshen Kemal After Two Heartbreaking Miscarriages

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, July 19, 2024

British singer-songwriter Simon Solomon Webbe AKA Simon Webbe, who started his professional musical journey in 2000, is currently over the moon nine. His wife Ayshen has given birth after they suffered their first loss in February 2019.

Today, on deck, get to know in detail about Simon Webbe’s married life, heartbreaking loss, and children. Also, get to know more about his past affairs and relationships:

Simon and Wife Ayshen Welcome First Child Together

British rapper Simon has announced the arrival of his first child with his wife, Ayshen. The 45 years old singer could not stop posting his joy on Instagram page on Tuesday. The singer revealed that they were in a perfect bubble after the arrival of their first child.

Simon posted a black and white picture with his wife writing: We’re so blessed to announce we have a new arrival. In the same post, the singer claimed that the new mother is in recovery, whereas he is rushing off his feet.

He wrote:

His Blue bandmate Antony Costa was the first one to wish him congratulations in the comments section. Even though the singer did not reveal the gender of the newborn, he in the caption hinted that they welcomed a little boy.

In January, Simon announced that they were expecting a baby after two miscarriages. His wife, talent manager, immediately fell pregnant after they took a break from family planning. The duo suffered their first loss in February 2019 and again suffered a miscarriage a year later.

Well, besides this, Simon is also father to daughter Alanah that he welcomed from his previous relationship with shop assistant Nicola Jones.

Simon Webbe Married Life And Relationships

Simon is currently happily married to wife, Ayshen Kemal. The lovely couple tied the knot back in August 2018 in Corinthia Hotel in London. Now, they enjoy nearly three years-long conjugal life together.

Simon’s bandmates Lee Ryan, Antony Costa, and Duncan James were present at the ceremony. Talking about the marriage, the singer claimed that he wanted to keep his wedding private, thus banned photos from the event.

There were rumors that Simon had cheated on his husband with a blond girl just days before celebrating their first anniversary.

Simon proposed Ayshen during Valentine’s Day in 2017 during their Jamaican getaway. During the proposal, they were dating for three years. The duo first met at a celebrity football match years ago, but Ayshen had a boyfriend during the time.

Over a decade long, they crossed paths and fell for each other, realizing that they were made for one another.

Well, talking about his past affairs, he has dated Natalie Denning, Stacey McIntosh, Layla Manoochenri, and Maria Kouka. He also has welcomed daughter Alanah with girlfriend Nicola Jones.
