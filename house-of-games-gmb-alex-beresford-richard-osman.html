House of Games viewers all have the same complaint as GMB's Alex Beresford joins celeb line-up

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, April 14, 2024

HOUSE of Games viewers were all left complaining after Good Morning Britain star Alex Beresford joined the line-up.

Every week four new famous faces join host Richard Osman to play a host of different games to win the coveted prize.

On Monday, the celebrity contestants taking on the quiz included Matthew Horne, Martel maxwell, Chloe Petts and Alex, 41.

While the TV presenter looked pleased to be taking part in the show - viewers at home were less than delighted to see him taking part.

"Couldn't they have found anyone better than fame hungry Alex Beresford?" one slammed as the programme started.

A second chimed in: "Had to turn off House of Games as hate seeing that moron Alex Beresford on TV yet again.
