DANIEL STURRIDGE flashed his brand new gold gnashers as he arrived at a popular Liverpool nightclub to celebrate Nathaniel Clyne's 26th birthday bash on Sunday.

The Liverpool striker, who rocked up to Merseyside's Playground nightclub with teammate Georginio Wijnaldum, was also hanging out with British rappers Tinie Tempah and Grime star Stormzy.

Sturridge is said to run the rule over his Liverpool team-mates' style but his choices on Sunday were questionable.

The England international completed his look with several gold chains, watch, earrings, a trademark nose stud and stylish glasses.
