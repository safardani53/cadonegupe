Nicki Minaj’s Curly Hair

This is our first time seeing Nicki’s natural hair worn curly — we think. Now, the rap diva is definitely the queen of all weaves, so we could be deceived, but it looks super natural!

We can’t believe Nicki hasn’t tried this style sooner! We love how she has been wearing more subtle, natural looking hairstyles. Of course, her hair was accompanied by her signature winged eyeliner, subtle golden eyeshadow, and pretty pink lipstick.

While we’re still obsessing over Nicki’s curly hair, the rap mogul has been getting ready to host and perform at the European Music Awards. With the success of her recent singles and the new hosting gig in the works, who knows what Nicki will do next?

Whatever is it, we hope we’ll get to see her debut more hot hairstyles!
