Cliff Emmich dead at 85 as fans pay tribute to actor who starred in Little House on the Prairie, Hal

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, April 18, 2024

HALLOWEEN II actor Cliff Emmich has died at age 85 due to an on-going health battle.

Emmich's rep confirmed his tragic death with Fox News Digital and said that the star suffered from lung cancer.

"My client and friend for over fifty years lost his battle with lung cancerâ€¦ He passed at his home in Valley Village on Monday," his rep said in a statement.

Emmich was born in Cincinnati, Ohio, but grew up in Los Angeles. California.

He has been in more than 90 films and tv programs since his film debut in the 1969's Gaily, Gaily.

He is popularly known for his roles in Payday, Little House on the Prairie, and Thunderbolt and Lightfoot to name a few.

Those who know the actor best for his role as the character Mr. Garrett in Halloween II took their sentiments to Twitter, including the verified Halloween Verse account.

"Cliff Emmich, our Mr. Garrett from Halloween 2 fame, has sadly passed away," Halloween Verse said in a tweet.

The Halloween account included hashtags in the touching tweet like #MrGarrett, #RIP and #Halloween.

"Fantastic character actor," a fan said in reply to the tweet.
