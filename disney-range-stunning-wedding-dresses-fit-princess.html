Disney is bringing out a range of stunning wedding dresses and they are fit for a princess

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, March 10, 2024

EVER dreamed of having your own fairytale wedding?

A bridal boutique has announced it is collaborating with Disney to bring out some jaw-dropping gowns themed on the popular princesses. 

The Allure Bridal collection includes dresses themed on Belle, Ariel, Tiana, Rapunzel, Snow White, Sleeping Beauty and Cinderella. 

There will be 16 styles in total and they have been designed to “capture the style and spirit of Disney Princess characters". 

They will include a variety of silhouettes and fabrics, from tulle, organza, lace and chiffon.

The bridal collection will be unveiled during New York Bridal Fashion Week in April 2020, and will be available to buy shortly after. 

The dresses will be range from $1,200 (£919) to $2,500 (£1,916), although seven of the 16 styles will be part of Disney Fairy Tale Weddings Platinum Collection that is priced up to £7,664.

Sadly the collection will be available at select bridal boutiques across North America, so you may need to grab your magic flying carpet across the pond to get your hands on one. 

You will be able to find the Disney Fairy Tale Weddings Platinum Collection at Kleinfeld Bridal stores in New York and Toronto.

Allure Bridals CEO Kelly Crum said: “So many brides grow up admiring their favourite Disney Princess characters and are inspired by their journeys, gowns and stories of the classic films.

“Our design team worked tirelessly on each of these incredible gowns, as each dress features intricate detailing specifically inspired by the timeless characters known and loved by all of us at Allure and Kleinfeld, as well as our brides. 

“We are honoured to work with Disney on this collection and see the romance of it come to life."
