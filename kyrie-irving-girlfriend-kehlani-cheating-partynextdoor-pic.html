Is Kyrie Irvings Girlfriend, Kehlani, Cheating On Him With PartyNextDoor? Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, March 14, 2024

“After all her shenanigans, still got the R&B singer back in my bed,” PND wrote on a March 28 Instagram picture that he quickly deleted. PND’s pic didn’t show Kehlani’s face, just two hands locked together, per Complex. However, unless there’s another woman out there with a WOKE tattoo on her hand, just like Kehlani, it’s pretty obvious that PND and his R&B ex were sharing the same bed. You better believe that the Cavs fans weren’t happy with this.

Uh-oh. It looks like the “Nothing But Net” rapper just stole Kehlani from the Cavs pointguard. Kyrie and Kehlani made their romance Instagram official in January 2016, according to Complex, with Kehlani reportedly calling her basketball bae “a king.” She also said that “you’ve shown me so much in so little time.. lost faith in alotta things, but lots have been restored.” Get to know more about Kehlani here and if you want to know more about PARTYNEXTDOOR, click here. While romance may fade over time, music is forever so

Before Kyrie came into the picture, PND and Kehlani were like Chris Brown, 26, and Karrueche Tran, 27, in that they were on-again/off-again lovers. PND seemed to close the door on the relationship with he dropped “Kehlani’s Freestyle,” per The Source. The rapper, who later changed the song to, “Things & Such,” confirmed that the relationship was over while also admitting his “missteps.”

In the wake of the cuddling photo, Kehlani went ahead and outright deleted her Instagram page. Meanwhile, Kyrie still has some pics of Kehlani on his account. On March 11, he even posted a shot of them together with the caption “Good ole fashioned partners.” Uh, awkward. Well, on the bright side, Kyrie’s going to have some free time to help LeBron James, 31, and the rest of the Cleveland Cavaliers make the NBA Playoffs.
