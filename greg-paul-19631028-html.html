Gregory Scott Paul is an American freelance scholar, author and illustrator working in the field of paleontology, and recently studied sociology and theology. He is known for his work and research on arthropods and his detailed illustrations, both live and skeletal. Professionally investigating and restoring dinosaurs for three decades, Paul has earned on-screen credit as the Discovery Channel's Jurassic Park and When Dinosaurs Roamed America and Dinosaur Planet expert. He is the author and illustrator of Dinosaurs World, A Complete Illustrative Guide to Dinosaur Skeletons, Aerial Dinosaurs, Princeton Field Dinosaurs Guide, Coffee Table Book Dinosaurs by Gregory S. Paul, and is the editor of the Scientific American Book of Dinosaurs . Paul's recent research on the interaction between religion and society has been covered by the international press and media.
