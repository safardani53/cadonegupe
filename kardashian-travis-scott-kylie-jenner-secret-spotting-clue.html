Kardashian fans think Travis Scott &amp; Kylie Jenner are keeping a huge secret after spotting blink-and

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, June 6, 2024

TRAVIS Scott has fans convinced that he and Kylie Jenner are married after they noticed a telling detail in a new photo.

The rapper might have leaked the news while posing for a mirror selfie.

On Monday, Travis took to his Instagram Stories to share a solo snap where he appeared to be in his bedroom.

The Goosebumps rapper was shirtless, sporting just baggy black pants and a white belt while putting his body ink on full display.

He covered his face with his cell phone, but one object, in particular, stood out to fans in the background.

A black and white photo sat on the shelf behind him, appearing to be a bride and groom sharing a kiss.

Upon further inspection, fans thought the couple in the photo might be Travis and Kylie, fueling rumors that they took the next step in their relationship.

One eagle-eyed fan shared the snap on an online thread, zooming in on the framed snap.

They captioned the post: "Travis + Kylie wedding photo?"

Commenters were divided on who the mystery couple could be in the pic, with some believing the girl could be their 4-year-old daughter, Stormi.

One user asked: "Am I trippin' or is that Stormi?"

One theorized: "Or it could just be a shoot they did. It seems they take family photos but don’t share them."

Another remarked: "They both like the thirst traps these days."

WEDDING BELLS

In July, Travis, 31, and Kylie, 25, sparked engagement rumors after fans stumbled upon what they believed was the pair's wedding registry.

A list called "MOTHER GOOSE and CACTUS JACK's Registry" was uncovered, full of staggeringly expensive items.

The registry was dated for a wedding on December 10, 2022.

After the list was shared on an online thread, its title was changed to "?? ???? and ???? JACK's Registry."

Fans pointed out that Stormi often calls Kylie Mother Goose, and she has used it in several posts.

Meanwhile, Cactus Jack is the name of Travis' brand for his merchandise.

The URL for the page is "jackgoose1."

Other fans claimed it was a "dead giveaway" that the list is full of items of luxury furniture priced up to $500,000.

The people behind the registry are asking for items like a $500,000 Baccarat Zenuth chandelier, a $390,000 Daum crystal tropical peacock, a $255,000 Lui Lei armoire, and a $75,0000 Meissen sea life chess set.

A fan wrote: "The dead giveaway is the TICKET PRICE some of these Knick knacks come with, dayum!

Who else would be able to afford these gifts but their friends & family?"

Kylie and Travis have previously talked about getting married, with the rap star saying in 2018 that he wants to pop the question.

He told Rolling Stone: "We'll get married soon. I just gotta sturdy up — I gotta propose in a fire way."

At the time, the duo were only parents to their daughter but have since welcomed their second child, a baby boy born in February 2022.

NAME GAME

Neither Kylie nor Travis has yet to release the name of their son or post photos of his face.

The pair initially announced that his name was Wolf but retracted the moniker shortly afterward, claiming it "didn't fit him."

Many think the switch was due to drama between Kylie's former BFF, Tammy Hembrow, whose 6-year-old son also has the name Wolf.

However, Kylie explained during an appearance on  The Late Late Show with James Corden earlier this month that she hasn't made any legal changes just yet.

"It's still Wolf. His password's still Wolf," the young mom admitted, though she plans to change that.

"But that isn't going to be his name. We're just waiting," she said.

"How long are you going to wait?" the talk show host asked.

Kylie explained that Travis could not decide and continued to change his mind about their son's name.
