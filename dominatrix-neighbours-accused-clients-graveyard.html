We're fed up with our dominatrix neighbour - she takes clients to a GRAVEYARD near our homes

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, May 19, 2024

NEIGHBOURS of a dominatrix say they're fed up with her taking clients to a graveyard near their homes.

Dominatrix Leigh, from Swindon, admitted taking clients to the cemetery but denied spanking them there.

On her website, where she refers to herself as 'the Governess', Leigh invites clients to her 'orphanage'.

She offers 'nurturing sessions' which include 'feeding, bath times, lots of cuddles, story time, or perhaps a nice movie with me while snuggled on the sofa'.

But now, outraged neighbours claim she has started taking her clients to a nearby cemetery in front of shocked schoolkids and dog walkers.

Leigh denied allegations by neighbours that she spanked clients there, saying they are "absolute rubbish" and she has only "taken people through there" to walk to town.

One neighbour, who wanted to remain anonymous, told The Sun: "I would say this was two weeks ago. The wife came in and said 'you wouldn't believe what's going on'.

"She's only taken a client around to the cemetery. She's actually doing it in front of the other dog walkers.

"The chap likes to get bent over the bench and she hits him, spanks him, with a cane. 

"She's actually asked the dog walkers 'I'm going to do something today. Would you mind if I do it?' They said 'we don't want to be a part of it'.

"They were conveniently at the top of the cemetery while she was in the middle over the bench with her client and he likes people to watch. 

"The next thing we hear is one of them goes around in a pink tutu in the cemetery with her."

In response, Leigh said: "The spanking in a cemetery is absolute rubbish. I use the cemetery to take my dog for a walk; I use it morning and evening.

"I have taken people [clients] through there if I've been walking through to town or walking to the shop, that is it.

"We've only ever used the footpaths; we've not been near any of the graves. We're not stupid people, it would be disrespectful [to spank clients there]."

Leigh appeared on TV show Stacey Dooley Sleeps Over alongside 'Britain's oldest dominatrix' Sherry Lever, 71 - otherwise known as 'Mistress Sofia'.

On the show, Dominatrix Leigh was introduced as a 'novice' by Dooley.

The group were 'summoned to a play room' in Sherry's family conservatory for a caning lesson.
