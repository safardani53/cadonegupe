Where to buy dog Halloween costumes

Your best bet for buying dog costumes, in general, is to go online because there is much more choice than you'll find in stores.

Many pet-oriented retailers offer fun selections of Halloween-themed costumes, so here's a handful to help you get started with the search.

How much do dog Halloween costumes cost?

Getting your pooch ready for Halloween won't cost you an arm and a leg, even for the more detailed costumes.

If you want something no fuss and simple, it'll only set you back around £5 for some of the cheaper options like the jumpers.

If, however, you want to go all out with something like the spider costume or make your pal a superhero, it's around the £30 mark, and that's usually the highest end cost-wise.

How to make a dog Halloween costume?

If you don't want to actually buy a costume, you could try and finesse a classic by chopping up a pillowcase and creating eye holes for a ghost.

Or, if you want to get really creative, you can always create your own spider or bat costume out of pipe cleaners.

Just make sure that your homemade outfit is safe and has no sharp bits, or they'll be in for a horrible trick come Halloween.
