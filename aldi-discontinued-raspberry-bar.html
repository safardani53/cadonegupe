Aldi confirms fan-favorite candy is discontinued but offers shoppers much-needed hope

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 6, 2024

ALDI fans have called out the discount retailer after it discontinued a popular candy treat.

The Raspberry Bar made its way into the store’s Aldi Finds section temporarily, but shoppers adored the sweet so much they’re calling for it to make a comeback.

With just one gram of sugar per serving, each chocolate bar also boasted 7 grams of protein in just 90 calories.

One shopper asked Aldi on Twitter why the raspberry bar had disappeared from stores.

“This was actually an ALDI Finds item!” the company replied in a tweet.

While being an ALDI Finds item means the product is available only on a temporary basis, Aldi offered shoppers a little bit of hope though.

“No plans for a return just yet, but we hope we can see it again in stores one day,” the retailer responded to the initial comment.

MORE ALDI DISAPPOINTMENTS

This isn’t the first time Aldi has disappointed shoppers after removing popular products from stores.

The iconic Pickle de Gallo, which was a salsa made out of pickles, also disappeared from the retailer.

Many customers used it for dipping, spreading, or as a topping on sandwiches or hot dogs.

After shoppers persistently asked Aldi where the spread went, the store replied on Twitter: "No return date to share just yet, but we will try to talk our buying team into making it come back!"

The popular Fremont Fish Market’s crab cakes also disappeared from stores this summer.

"AldiUSA where are the crab cakes?!?!?" tweeted one distraught customer who had been searching the Indianapolis area for weeks.

Luckily, Aldi had some positive news to share.

"Okay, okay! The wait is almost over. They'll be back in stores on 7/19," Aldi said in response.

The store’s popular lava cake dessert also got the boot, as Aldi confirmed it was winding down production of the treat.

One disappointed shopper wrote on Reddit: “No exaggeration, these were the best things I’ve ever had from Aldi!”

Another one said of the treat: “If you want a little something decadent after dinner I would grab these and enjoy with a nice glass of wine.”
