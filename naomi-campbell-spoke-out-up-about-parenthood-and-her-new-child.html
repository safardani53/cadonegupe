Naomi Campbell Spoke Out Up About Parenthood And Her New Child

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 20, 2024

In an interview with British Vogue, the 51-year-old supermodel confessed that her nine-month-old daughter is not adopted, contrary to popular belief due to her young age.

“She is not an adoptive kid; she is my child.”

Campbell kept her baby’s name a secret and claimed that just a few people knew she was expecting a child. Later in the interview, she described her daughter as the “greatest gift” and “best thing” she has ever done. Campbell also stated in the interview that she still enjoys modeling but has become “nerve-wracking” as she gets older.

“Because I’m 51 years old and I’m strolling with 18-year-old females!” It’s fantastic to be among these young ‘uns my age, but during one of the gigs, I was like, “Come on, gals! Get your feet up! “Why are you moving so slowly?”

Campbell’s pregnancy rumors arose after her appearance at New York Fashion Week in 2021, when she walked the catwalk without a prominent baby bulge. She revealed the birth of her daughter weeks after the program. Campbell came to Instagram in May 2021 to announce the birth of her baby. She called her kid a “wonderful little gift” and claimed she was “proud” to be a mother in a now-deleted post.

Naomi Campbell encouraged her older friends to have children.

Naomi Campbell admitted in the same interview that she had wanted to have a kid for a long time. She said she always wanted to be a mother and considers herself “fortunate” to have a daughter. Naomi described her attitude to parenthood as “totally altruistic,” and she didn’t mind the age difference between her and her kid. She even acknowledged urging her older pals to have children.

“I’m urging them every one to go for it! Don’t be hesitant!”

When asked how parenthood has impacted her outlook on life, Campbell said that she now feels “like a child again” since she sees the world through her daughter’s eyes. According to the supermodel, motherhood has taught her that her kid comes first and that everything she does is for her child.
