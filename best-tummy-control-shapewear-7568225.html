The 20 Best Tummy Control Shapewear of 2024

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, May 13, 2024

Shapewear adds a layer of compression to the body and is often worn under tight-fitting garments to provide a smoothing and shaping effect. While it has a reputation of cinching and sculpting, shapewear does not have to feel uncomfortable, especially when wearing the compressive undergarment to target more sensitive areas such as the stomach. Instead, tummy control options should feel comfortable and supportive while delivering all of the amazing benefits of shapewear. 

With targeted compression in the midsection, tummy control shapewear is designed to help shape, smooth, and enhance the stomach area. “People might want to purchase this type of shapewear to achieve a streamlined, flat look under fitted clothing or to boost their confidence in tighter, body-hugging outfits,” Susie Taaffe, the founder of Skanties Anti-Shapewear, tells PEOPLE. 

From celebrities such as Kim Kardashian (and her amazing Skims shapewear line) to fashion moguls like Gigi Hadid, tummy control shapewear is a fun and functional tool to add to your wardrobe for special events and everyday wear. We scoured the internet for the best tummy control shapewear, carefully looking at various styles (including underwear and bike shorts), fabric flexibility, and more to determine which shapewear is actually worth buying.

Keep reading to discover more about the best tummy-control shapewear of 2024.

How to Pick the Right Shapewear

Targeted Compression

When shopping for shapewear designed for specific purposes such as the tummy area, it’s important to look for designs with targeted compression so that you can get adequate support and shaping. “Targeted compression styles strategically apply pressure to specific areas, aiding in shaping and smoothing specific areas of the body,” says Gabrielle Richards, Brand Director for Shapermint. “If you’re looking for a shaping garment to shape or smooth a specific area, go for a style that targets the body part you’d prefer to smooth or enhance.” For tummy control shapewear, consider comfortable garments designed to target the belly, waist, and hip area. 

Stretch

The best shapewear are supposed to have some compression and resistance to it; however, you still want to be able to move. With this in mind, Sheila Attari, the founder and CEO of SheBird, says to look for flexible fabrics and designs. “Look for material that has a four-way stretch to keep you comfortable all day,” she explains. At the end of the day, you want the shapewear to do its job but you also want to be comfortable, which is why stretch is super important. For stretchier styles, consider the Wolford Tulle Forming Underbust Shaper Thong Bodysuit which has over 30 percent stretch and is designed with plenty of comfortable flexibility so you can feel supported while still being able to move. 

Comfortable Fabric

In addition to stretch, Richards says to make sure the fabric in general is comfortable, breathable, and not restrictive. “Nylon and spandex are key shapewear fabrics that deliver on stretch and flexibility so you’ll feel comfortable all day long,” she explains. Most nylon shapewear fabrics have moisture-wicking properties, too, which is ideal for warmer climates (or year round if you tend to run hot).  

For a comfortable nylon-based shapewear style, consider the Shapermint High Waisted Body Shaper Shorts. The Skims Core Control High-Waisted Thong — which is our best everyday pick — is also constructed from a stretchy and breathable nylon. 

Supportive Features 

“A well-designed shapewear item will feature supportive features such as soft boning to provide support for posture and keep the garment from rolling down,” says Richards. In addition to boning, she says to look for silicone along the waistband or leg hems so that the material grips to the body and to prevent the material from rolling down. The Skims Core Control High-Waisted Thong features a band like this, as does the Yitty Headliner Shaping High Waist Short.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What shapewear really works?

    We did the research and tapped experts when putting together this list of the best tummy control shapewear. The Skims Core Control High-Waisted Thong features a comfortable yet compressive high waist band designed to support and shape the stomach area. For a more budget-friendly option, we recommend the Jockey Slimmers Breathe High-Rise Brief, which also has a waist cinching design for additional support and shaping. If tummy control underwear isn’t your thing, the Shapermint High Waisted Body Shaper Shorts is another excellent option as it features a compressive design and comfortable, breathable material. For an open-bust style, the Shapermint Open Bust Shaper Camisole has similar shaping benefits as an open-bust one-piece without any potential restrictions caused by the bodysuit style.

  • Should you buy shapewear a size bigger?

    At first, shapewear in your usual size might feel a little tight — this is on purpose. Since shapewear is compressive, it should fit snug against the body. To ensure you have the right fit, Richards says to always purchase your usual size. “Purchasing it in too big of a size can reduce the shaping and smoothing features,” she explains. “I also do not recommend purchasing shapewear a size smaller as it can be uncomfortable and restricting.”

  • What's the difference between tummy tucker and shapewear?

    According to Richards, some use tummy tucker and shapewear interchangeably. “A tummy tucker targets the abdomen whereas shapewear is the category of garments that a tummy tucker is within,” she explains, adding that there are many different types of tummy control shapewear, including tummy tuckers, shaping panties, shaping shorts, and shaping tops.
