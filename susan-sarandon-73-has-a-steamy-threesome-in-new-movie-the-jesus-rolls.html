Susan Sarandon, 73, has a steamy THREESOME in new movie The Jesus Rolls The Sun

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, March 25, 2024

ACTRESS Susan Sarandon is known for her sexy scenes in movies and her upcoming movie, The Jesus Rolls, promises to deliver yet another one.

The 73-year-old bombshell has a steamy threesome scene with actors John Turturro and Bobby Cannavale in the new crime comedy The Jesus Rolls.

The Jesus Rolls is being released on February 28 in the United States and on March 20 in the UK.

The movie is both a remake of the 1974 French film Going Places by Bertrand Blier and a spin-off to the 1998 cult film The Big Lebowski by the Coen brothers.

John reprises his role as Jesus Quintana in The Jesus Rolls, where he is also the director and the writer.

During the movie, his character jumps in bed with Susan after her character Jean is released from prison and she celebrates by having a threesome.

In the clip, the actress is pinned between the two men while they shower her with attention.

In one shot, Susan's character has her arm wrapped around Jesus while Petey, the other man, undresses her.

Another clip shows him grabbing her breast as she continues to undress.

She also ends up in bed between the two friends.

Susan is no stranger to these types of scenes as she previously starred in the cult-favorite movie, The Rocky Horror Picture Show where she played Janet Weiss.

Her character spends half of the movie in her bra and the other half in black lingerie. She starred beside Tim Curry and Barry Bostwick.

The Oscar-winning actress has also participated in a lesbian sex scene in 1983 with The Hunger.

When filming this particular 80's hit, she talked about the process of filming the shot.

She said: "For The Hunger, the whole point of that scene between Catherine and me is that she transfuses her, in order to get her addicted to blood. It's not just a love scene.

“And so the love scene itself was never really made explicit. For me, what's interesting about love scenes is the getting into it and what happens after."

She also starred next to Michelle Pfeiffer and Cher while the three lounged in lingerie in The Witches of Eastwick.

The actress has also previously talked about her sexuality.

She told Pridesorce.com: "My sexual orientation is up for grabs, I guess you could say.”

She also talked about how even though her sexual orientation is "up for grabs", she's a monogamist.

She said: "I haven’t really had a large dating career. I haven’t exactly been in the midst of a lot of offers of any kind. I’m still not!

“I don’t know what’s going on! But I think back in the ’60s it just was much more open.”
