All The Golden Bachelor Contestants Ages and Instagrams

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, April 27, 2024

What are the contestants’ ages and Instagram on ‘The Golden Bachelor’? Get to know all the women competing for Gerry Turner’s heart.

There is no denying that Gerry Turner is one lucky man! He is the very first Golden Bachelor and 71-year-old Gerry is certainly a catch.

Despite concerns from fans initially that Gerry's women might be much younger than him, it looks like the network opted for a group of very age-appropriate (and age-defying) contestants.
