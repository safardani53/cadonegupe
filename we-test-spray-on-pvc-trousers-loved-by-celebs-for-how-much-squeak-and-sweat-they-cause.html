We test 'spray-on' PVC trousers loved by celebs for how much squeak and sweat they cause

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, February 18, 2024

SPRAY-ON trousers are one of spring’s hottest – and sweatiest – trends.

High-shine, skintight styles have been seen on celebrities including Kate Beckinsale, Kylie Jenner and Kim Kardashian.

In fact, fashion has developed a serious fetish for the dominatrix look. For those planning to get glam, we tried out some high street efforts.

Model Tinna Bergs, 34, tells us how she got on and gives each pair a talc rating out of ten – with one being squeak-free and ten needing tons of powder.

Tinna says: “These were a nightmare. They stuck to my legs when putting them on and were so tight they show up every lump and bump. A serious case of VPL.”
