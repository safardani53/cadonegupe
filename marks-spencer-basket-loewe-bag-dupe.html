Marks &amp; Spencer's 30 dupe of Loewe's straw basket bag is the perfect summer holdall- and it's back

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, April 14, 2024

M&S are selling the ultimate summer bag, and we're obsessed.

Their £30 basket bag is the perfect high street alternative to designer Loewe's basket bags.

Available to shop now at marksandspener.com, the Loewe dupe is sure to be hot property.

With Loewe's iconic handbag frequently seen on the arm of influencers and celebrities alike, M&S' lookalike is a great chance to get your hands on the bag for less.

And with the Loewe original costing £400, M&S' £30 option is much more palatable.

Its available in a cute mini size for £29.50, and a larger Straw Tote Bag for £39.50.

Inside there's a cotton drawstring pouch and zip pocket to keep your possessions safe, and on the outside you'll find the signature straw design with leather look handles and patch.

It's available in three gorgeous colourways, including a natural tan, white and yellow.

With Loewe's totes costing from £375 for the white bag, the M&S dupe is sure to be the bag of the summer.

Just don't hang about, it won't be on the shelves for long.
