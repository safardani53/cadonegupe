John Fanta Wikipedia And Age: All About College Basketball Journalist

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, July 8, 2024

We have decided to create a John Fanta Wikipedia with his age since fans have been keen to know more about him. Read till the end.

Fanta is an emerging sports broadcaster who will make big names for himself.

This article contains information about his age, educational background, career, and wife.

A passionate basketball journalist, John Fanta has been in the media business since a young age. On top of his knowledge of basketball, he is well known for his humor and choice of outfits

He is a play-by-play announcer for Fox Sports and hosts Big East Shootaround, a weekly digital show that airs on Big East Digital Network, Twitter, and YouTube.

Fanta knew exactly what he wanted to do since his high school days. Despite being a student, Fanta always kept his dream alive by actively engaging in sports journalism.

He attended Saint Ignatius High School, where he broadcasted around 200 different sporting events. Fanta has extensive knowledge about the contest’s main characters.

Moreover, he covered current Seton Hall sports teams as a student broadcaster and the voice of the Pirate Sports Network.

Unlike other reporters, Fanta is well-known for covering both men’s and women’s sports.

Let’s Talk About John Fanta Wikipedia And Age

The age of Fanta was reportedly born in the year 1995, making his 29 years old as of 2024. However, his exact date of birth is not disclosed.

Hailing from Hoboken, New Jersey, Fanta has gained numerous broadcasting experience over the years.

His Education

After completing high school at Saint Ignatius, he also obtained a Bachelor of Arts (BA) in Journalism from Seton Hall University in 2017.

Fanta surprisingly managed to maintain an amazing 3.75 GPA as a senior while working side by side as the university’s journalist.

He was supervising a group of 30 students in the Pirate Sports Network, which broadcasted more than 50 Seton Hall home sporting events each year.

He also worked as a sportscaster and news anchor for Seton Hall’s student radio station, 89.5 WSOU.

Aside from his journalism, Fanta also has religious zeal. He served the campus community as a lector and cantor at weekly masses.

Fanta’s Career

Fanta made his national television debut on FOX Sports in 2015. Since then, he has made many appearances between FOX Sports 1 and FOX Sports 2.

Fanta has been covering a variety of college sports, including basketball, soccer, baseball, and softball. He has been a FOX College Hoops Play-by-Play Broadcaster since November 2018. 

Prior to this, John worked with the BIG EAST Conference for nine years. He also holds numerous positions for BIG EAST Shootaround.

Moreover, in 2016, he served as a Sports Production Intern for FOX Sports, where he produced highlight packages for MLB Whiparound and foreign soccer coverage.

John Fanta’s Wife, Victoria Rizzo Fanta

Fanta is happily married to his loving wife, Victoria Rizzo Fanta, a registered dental hygienist. The pair tied the knot on September 1, 2023.

The wedding website of the pair says that their story is magical, made for fairytales. Fanta was not traveling around for his broadcasting job due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

So, since he was celebrating Christmas with the Campisano family, he had gone out to get a Christmas tree. It was then that the pair had met for the first time.

Breaking the ice, John asked if Victoria was single, and within a few minutes of talking, they set a date.

Fanta finally proposed on May 13, 2022, and shared their vows a year later. Moreover, the pair has not birthed any child as of yet. 

They love spending time with each other and frequently go out for recreational activities such as horse riding.
