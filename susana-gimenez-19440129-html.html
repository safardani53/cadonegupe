María Susana Giménez Aubert, known as Susana Giménez, is an Argentine TV presenter, actress, model and businesswoman. In 2012, she was considered the most popular person on Argentine television by the media company that published its magazine of the same name. She is the host of Susana Giménez, a critically acclaimed Argentine variety show similar in format to Raffaella Carrà and Oprah Winfrey. In 1997 she received the Martín Fierro Gold Award and in 2002 the INTE Award for TV Host of the Year.
