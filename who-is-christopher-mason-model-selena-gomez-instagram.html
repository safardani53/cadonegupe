Who Is Christopher Mason? 5 Things To Know About Selena Gomezs Model Crush Hollywood Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, May 10, 2024

1. He’s a model

Chris is a Los Angeles-based model, signed to Wilhelmina Models, and he is not shy about showing off his sexy shots on Instagram. From shirtless shots to moody selfies, his page is full of pics of him lookin’ g-o-o-d. Oh, and get this — he started off his career posing for Hollister, LOL!

2. Mason isn’t his real last name

The Cali boy actually changed his name to begin his modeling career — his real monkier is Chris Brown! Yep, just like that singer you’ve probably heard of. Since he’s in the industry, it was probably a smart move to differentiate himself.

3. He has a daughter

Chris is constantly posting photos of his adorable toddler on his social media page, and although it’s not 100% clear, it definitely doesn’t seem like he’s still with the little girl’s mother. She is just SOOO cute though! In one shot, he even revealed he’s trying to get his offspring started in modeling — he took her to a Baby Gap shoot.

4. He has very artistic interests

Besides modeling, Chris lists his favorite hobbies as “more music,design, photography, cuisine literature and half naked women” on his Tumblr page. And, yes, all of those things are 100% evident on his personalized site, he doesn’t lie! Plus, he also has a furniture design company called Andrew Mason Design, and says that career is what he’d be doing full time if he hadn’t pursued modeling.

5. He’s walked the runway a few times, too

Chris is 5’11”, which isn’t that tall in the modeling world, so he hasn’t done much work on the runway. However, his experience isn’t totally limited — he’s walked for Levi’s, 7 For All Mankind and more. Not too shabby!
