What time is Love Island on ITV2 tonight? Here's why you need to watch episode three

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 15, 2024

THERE is a lot of drama coming up tonight (June 5) in the Love Island villa after last night's re-coupling.

In a shock move, Tommy Fury stole Lucie Donlan from Joe Garratt - but will Lucie stay faithful to Joe? Here's why you have to watch the show this evening...

What time is Love Island on ITV2 tonight?

The show is back on tonight (June 5, 2019) at 9pm on ITV2, so don't forget to tune in to see if Lucie is being "loyal" to Joe.

If you miss any of the action - or can't believe what you're seeing and want to watch again - you can catch up on ITV2+1 .

You can also watch it on the ITV Hub after it has aired.

Don't miss out on any episodes - they'll all be on the Hub.

What will happen on tonight's Love Island?

Last night we saw Tommy pick Lucie at the end of the episode, stealing her from Joe.

Expect high drama tonight as the villa deals with the aftermath of this fallout.

Joe looked pretty annoyed and is likely to confront Tommy.

Amber Gill was also hoping Tommy would pick her, so she may confront him too.

In a sneak peek of tonight's show we saw Lucie and Tommy on a date and they both looked pretty happy.

Meanwhile, Joe was seen back at the villa looking worried, with Amber stirring the pot, by implying that Lucie had given Tommy reason to pick her.

We'll also see if new pairing Curtis Pritchard and Amy Hart hit it off.

Will one of the Islanders get sent home tonight?

Two of the boys could be at risk of elimination tonight as they aren't in a couple.

They are Joe and Anton Danyluk - as Tommy and Curtis pinched their girls.

However, maybe the girls will get to choose who they couple up with, which could see two other boys heading home.

Will any of the Islanders have sex on Love Island tonight?

It's very early days so we really doubt it, but you never know.

Lucie and Tommy could get down to business after their date, or maybe she'll stay on one of the outside beds with Joe?

But love is in the air so you never know what will happen.

MORE ON LOVE ISLAND

What days are Love Island on ITV2?

Summer has definitely arrived as Love Island has FINALLY landed on our TV screens!

Series five kicked off on Monday, June 3, 2019, at 9pm with a bumper launch show and the first couplings.

You’ll be able to get your fix of the show every night on ITV2 where the main programme, hilariously narrated by Iain Stirling,  will give us the lowdown on all the goings-on in the villa - from couples cracking on to singletons getting mugged off.

Saturday night's show differs from the rest of the week, as it is a round-up of all the best bits from the villa that week - and maybe some unseen footage!

If that’s not enough, you can also watch Caroline Flack's spin off show Aftersun on Sunday nights at 10pm after the main show has aired, where she discusses the latest action with celebrity guests, as well as those eliminated from the villa.

Like 2018, the show is likely to last eight weeks meaning the grand final could be around July 29, 2019.

Who will be this year’s winning couple? Stay tuned!
