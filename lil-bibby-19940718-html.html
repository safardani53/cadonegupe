Brandon Dickinson, better known by the stage name Lil Bibby, is an American rapper and its chief executive officer. Starting his career in 2011, Bibby released his first mixtape in 2013, titled Free Crack. He released the follow-up mixtape Free Crack 2 on August 29, 2014 and Free Crack 3 on November 27, 2015. Bibby announced in 2021 in an interview with VladTV that he has retired from rap in 2017 after going bankrupt. In 2017, he decided to switch to operating his own record company. He is also known for signing Juice WRLD for his studio Grade A Productions.
