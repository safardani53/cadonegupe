How Rich Is Ramona Singer? Net Worth, Career, Salary

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, April 15, 2024

Ramona Singer, star of Bravo’s RHONY (Real Housewives of New York City), will leave the show. After 13 seasons, the reality star has informed Page Six that she will not be returning to the program. Singer stated:

“I no longer want to perform the program.” It’s not for me right now.”

Singer has been in all 13 seasons of RHONY from 2008 to 2021 as one of the major cast members.

Ramona Singer’s net worth

Ramona Singer’s net worth is believed to be $16 million as of December 2023. She acquired her fortune from her television career as well as her ownership of RMS Fashions. She also created the Tru Renewal skincare brand and Ramona Singer Jewelry for the Home Shopping Network.

Singer debuted on RHONY in 2008, at the age of 52, with Jill Zaren, Kelly Bensimon, Alex McCord, and Bethenny Frankel. She allegedly earned $500,000 each episode of the program. She is the only cast member of the successful unscripted program to have appeared in all 13 seasons. RHONY Season 13 aired last year.

In terms of real estate, Singer allegedly leased out her Southampton property for $165,000 per month in 2018. Singer sold her 2,468-square-foot, four-bedroom, 3.5-bathroom Manhattan townhouse in July 2019 for little more than $4 million. She paid $1.42 million for the apartment with her ex-husband Mario Singer in 1998.

RHONY Ramona Singer is very committed to charitable activities.

Singer is a television personality, entrepreneur, real estate dealer, and novelist who was born in 1956. Ramona showed an interest in fashion at a young age and attended the Fashion Institute of Technology. She was the first graduate of the four-year honors program at the time, with a BS in marketing. She started her career as a buyer in Macy’s executive training program before moving on to work for prominent fashion brands such as French Connection and Calvin Klein.

Ramona Singer began her business path at the age of 30 when she launched her own firm, dealing in closeout products and collaborating with large stores. With the development of the HSN jewelry brand, Ramona Pinot Grigio, and True Faith jewelry, she then expanded into the wine and accessories industries.

Singer is also very committed to her charitable efforts. She often contributes a portion of her wine company profits to shelters for women and children who have been victims of domestic abuse. She is also active with the Africa Foundation/And Beyond, which helps construct schools for African children. Singer published her book Life on the Ramona Coaster in 2015. In 1992, Singer married Mario Singer, but the couple split in 2015. Her dating life was often portrayed on RHONY after her divorce. She was pictured with Steven Gerber at a charity event in 2017. However, after just one month of dating, they called it quits. Singer previously dated Tom D’Agostino in 2015.

Season 14 of RHONY will premiere next year.

The next season is slated to premiere in 2023. Although the show’s premiere date has yet to be confirmed, Andy Cohen revealed the identity of the seven Season 14 cast members on the last night of BravoCon 2022.

Influencer and content creator Sai De Silva, model and philanthropist Ubah Hassan, real estate agent Erin Dana Lichy, former J. Crew president and creative director Jenna Lyons, matchmaker and influencer Lizzy Savetsky, brand consultant and fashion publicist Jessel Taank, and marketing communications consultant Brynn Whitfield are among the Housewives who will appear in season 14. RHONY may be seen on Peacock and the Bravo app.
