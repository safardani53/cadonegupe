James Scully Biography

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, February 15, 2024

Born In San Antonio

James Scully was born as James Nicholas Scully on 19th April 1992 in San Antonio, Texas. He belongs to white ethnicity and holds American citizenship.

The actor also briefly lived in England as his father was posted there as the United States Air Force. He mostly spent his childhood in his home town in Texas. He was raised along with his sister Kate Scully-Ortega.

Scully first attended Driscoll Middle School and later studied at Lee High School's North East School of the Arts from where he graduated in 2010.

He later graduated with a Bachelor in musical theater from Otterbein University in Westerville, Ohio. 

Rose Fame From 'Heathers'

James Scully started his acting career from the television series in 2016. A year later he appeared in television show along with Jake Mclaughlin, Johanna Braddy, Aunjanue Ellis, and Priyanka Chopra.

He gained recognition after playing the role of JD in the 2018 television series alongside Grace Victoria Cox, Melanie Field, and Brendan Scannell.

 James Scully along with Grace Victoria Cox at the Launch Party of Heathers SOURCE: Getty Image

In 2019, he made his film debut from feature film . The actor also gave his voice for the Vidoe Game . The same year, he played the role of Forty Quinn in television series alongside Penn Badgley, Ambyr Childers, and Victoria Pedretti. 

Before becoming an actor, he worked three and a half years as a cycling instructor.
