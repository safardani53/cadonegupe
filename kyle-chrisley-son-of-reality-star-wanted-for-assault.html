Kyle Chrisley, Son of Reality Star, Wanted for Assault

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, April 17, 2024

Kyle Chrisley is a wanted man.

The 23-year old is the son of Chrisley Knows Best star Todd Chrisley and appeared sporadically on the first season of this USA Network reality show.

He’s accused of assaulting the mother of his child.

“Need Help to Locate Criminal Domestic Violence Suspect,” says a post on the South Carolina police department’s Facebook page this week, calling on the “public’s assistance in locating Kyle Hayden Chrisley (aka Kyle Crisley).”

 

Despite his appearances on Chrisley Knows Best, officials have been unable to track down Kyle for over a year now.

The oldest son of Todd Chrisley, Kyle is believed to have slapping the mother of his child and threatened her sister with a knife on February 24, 2013.

He is wanted on charges of Criminal Domestic Violence and Assault & Battery third degree.

You can actually watch Chrisley Knows Best online via TV Fanatic to see Kyle in action and call (864) 260-4415 or Crime Stoppers at 888-CRIMESC (274-6372) with information on his whereabouts.
