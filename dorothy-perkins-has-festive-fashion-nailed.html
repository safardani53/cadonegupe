Dorothy Perkins has festive fashion nailed

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 11, 2024

LOOKING for the perfect Christmas look? Dorothy Perkins has got the Wright party season outfits for you.

Last year's festive period was a wash out thanks to Covid so we've got a lot of making up to do and Dorothy Perkins is back to help with all your outfit needs to make sure your party season goes off with a bang.

Whether you’re looking to make a statement at the office Christmas party, or dress to the nines for Christmas dinner with the family then they have the look for you.

They may have disappeared from our high streets but the purse friendly brand is bursting with stylish and affordable looks online, so you’ll definitely find your new festive favourite.

This party season, they've teamed up with former Towie star Jess Wright for a 25 piece "Dress to Party" collection, the brands first celebrity face, which has all the essentials you'll need this season. From statement silhouettes, stand out fabrics and disco ball style embellishment, every piece in the range has a sprinkling of sparkle and Jess's signature style.

The edit also features iconic mini made for hitting the dancefloor, power suiting for making a statement and footwear you can actually wear to dance the night away.

Available in sizes 6 - 20 the show stopping Christmas essentials are guaranteed to make you look and feel amazing this season. The Jess Wright "Dress to Party" collection is available to shop from 10th November at DorothyPerkins.com.

Our Style Editor's top pick

STYLE Editor, Nana Acheampong, is loving Jess Wright's party collection at Dorothy Perkins.

"There's so much to choose from in the collection, but my top pick would be this sequin mini. It's great for the whole season because it can be styled up in loads of different ways with shoes, accessories and make up to create totally different looks for different parties!"
