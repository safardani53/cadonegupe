The Loud House Is Coming To Life In A New Live Action Series On Paramount+

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 12, 2024

Nickelodeon announces the brand new series, more Big Nate, a new Transformers series, and other new content!

Prepare the whole family for more fun, more laughter, and more action as the beloved Emmy-award winning series The Loud House springs to life in a whole new way!

A new live-action version of the series will begin filming its 10-episode first season this June in Albuquerque, New Mexico, with a premiere slated for later this year on Nickelodeon and Paramount+. As with the cartoon series, this new show follows the life of Lincoln Loud, an 11-year-old boy with 10 sisters, as he navigates everyday life while living with such a large family. Known as the “Man with a Plan,” Lincoln enlists the help of his best friend Clyde McBride to tackle the obstacles and mayhem that they encounter during their daily adventures.

STREAM: Full Episodes of The Loud House on Paramount+

The as-yet-untitled series follows the success of the live-action movie, A Loud House Christmas, with the real-life versions of the Loud family returning to reprise their roles, including Wolfgang Schaeffer, as Lincoln Loud, Jahzir Bruno as Clyde McBride, Brian Stepanek as Lynn Loud Sr., Sophia Woodward as Luna Loud, Catherine Ashmore Bradley as Luan Loud, Aubin Bradley as Lucy Loud, Ella Allan as Lola Loud, Mia Allan as Lana Loud, and Lexi Janicek as Lisa Loud. Additional casting news will be announced at a later date.

The news was announced at Nickelodeon’s annual upfront presentation, where network executives also shared the following exciting new content coming to Nickelodeon and Paramount+:

BIG NATE – The hit animated series will return for a second season with 10 new episodes. In Season 2, the hilarious and heart-filled hijinks that define the series will dive deeper into the backstories of Nate’s friends, introduce Nate’s grandparents, and more.

TRANSFORMERS – The brand-new animated action-comedy series Transformers: EarthSpark is set to premiere globally on Paramount+ in November. Produced in partnership between Nickelodeon Animation and Hasbro’s Entertainment One (eONE), the series introduces a new generation of Transformers—the first Transformer robots to be born on Earth—and together with the human family who adopts them, they'll redefine what it means to be a family. Following the launch of the series, the franchise will expand even further with a CG-animated theatrical film in 2024.

SPONGEBOB SQUAREPANTS — Bikini Bottom continues to expand with more episodes of thespinoff series Kamp Koral: SpongeBob’s Under Years. Plus, three character-driven movies will launch exclusively on Paramount+ with a fourth getting a theatrical release from Nickelodeon Animation, all currently in development.

BABY SHARK – The world of global pop culture phenomenon Baby Shark continues to grow with a feature-length movie in development for Paramount+ in the U.S., as well as a special featuring Grammy Award-winning, multiplatinum superstar Cardi B.

BLUE'S CLUES & YOU! – Upcoming highlights for the property include an original movie Blue’s Big City Adventure for Paramount+ and Nickelodeon, and a live tour Blue’s Clues & You! Live on Stage kicking off this fall in the U.S.
