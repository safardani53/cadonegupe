EastEnders legend Jacqueline Jossa relaxes with family as she hits the beach in swimsuit

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, May 9, 2024

EASTENDERS legend Jacqueline Jossa caught the eye in a stylish swimsuit as she hit the beach in Spain on a family holiday.

The actress, 30, soaked up some sun in the patterned one-piece which had a cut-out front that revealed a glimpse of midriff.

Jacqueline, who most famously played Lauren Branning in Enders, tied her brunette hair up and wore a white cap as she cooled off in the Mediterranean Sea.

The star looked happy as she stood on the sand in her sliders while holding her phone.

She managed to secure a parasol in a prime location, perfect for keeping the kids cool in the soaring Spanish heat.

Back in June, Jacqueline and husband Dan Osborne holidayed in Formentera, Spain, where they enjoyed a fun yacht day.

On that occasion, Jac made a splash in a vibrant orange bikini as she tried her hand at bodyboarding.

Not only is she a well-known actress, but Jac is also a dab hand when it comes to fashion and design.

She regularly wows her fans with snaps of her swimwear collections after teaming up with In The Style in a big money deal.

Speaking previously about posing in swimwear, Jac said: "They say self confidence is the best thing you can wear... but good swimwear always helps."

Jacqueline was snapped up by fast-fashion giant In The Style for a six-figure sum after winning I'm A Celebrity... Get Me Out of Here!

She released her own clothing line the following year and later admitted the show, which made her a millionaire, changed her life.

Speaking on The Sam and Billie Show, Jac admitted: "There’s been times where I haven’t had money. Before I went in the jungle I was struggling with money. I had to sell my house.

"And then I went in the jungle and had amazing opportunities, so I would never ever ever look at somebody and say, ‘They're not hustling enough’. You have to do it."
