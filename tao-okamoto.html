Career

Tao is a Japanese model and actress. She made her cinematic debut as the female protagonist, Mariko Yashida, in The Wolverine (2013). Okamoto also appeared in many episodes of Hannibal (2013), The Man in the High Castle (2015), and Westworld (2016). She decided to relocate to Paris in order to further her profession on a global scale. In January 2015, it was reported that Okamoto will co-star in the film Crossroads opposite Alodia Goseingfiao.

In the same year, she starred in the television series The Man in the High Castle as Betty Kasoura, the wife of lawyer Paul Kasoura and a client at Robert Childan’s American Artistic Handcrafts. She appeared as Merc Graves in the 2016 film Batman v Superman: Dawn of Justice. Tao began modeling as a youngster in Japan at the age of 14. Tao was named “Model of the Year” by the Japan Fashion Editors Club. In 2010, she was named one of Vogue Japan’s “Women of the Year.”

Tao has worked with Alexander Wang, Alexander McQueen, Burberry Prorsum, Bottega Veneta, Chanel, Dolce & Gabbana, Donna Karan, Emanuel Ungaro, Fendi, Giorgio Armani, Paul Smith, Max Mara, Miu Miu, Moschino, Paul Smith, Philip Lim, Ralph Lauren, Tommy Hilfiger, Kenzo, Maison Martin Margiela, Yves Saint Laurent, and others.
