Louis Brian Piccolo is an American professional footballer who returned to the Chicago Bears of the National Football League for four years. He died at the age of 26 from embryonic cell carcinoma, an aggressive form of testicular germ cell cancer, first diagnosed after it spread to his chest cavity. Piccolo was the subject of the 1971 Brian's Song television series, with a remake of the same name filmed in 2001. He was portrayed in the original James Caan and Sean Maher film in Remake of the Year. 2001.
