Happy anniversary, ‘General Hospital!’ The iconic soap is celebrating 60 years of storytelling. See photos of the epic anniversary special.

General Hospital has been a TV staple since it premiered all the way back in 1963. On January 4, 2024, ABC celebrated the show’s 60th anniversary with a special featuring cast members from the past and present.

The special included appearances by Maurice Benard, Jane Elliot, Genie Francis, Finola Hughes, Kelly Monaco, Laura Wright, and more. John Stamos, Val Chmerkovskiy, Emma Samms, ESPN’s Stephen A. Smith, Rick Springfield, Amber Tamblyn, and more celebrities made special appearances. Scroll down to see all the photos from General Hospital: 60 Years of Stars and Storytelling.
