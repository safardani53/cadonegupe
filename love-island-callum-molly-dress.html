Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, June 21, 2024

LOVE Island's Callum left partner Molly in hysterics as he strutted around in a DRESS.

The 23-year-old scaffolder showed off his playful side as some ex-Islanders visited the I Saw it First HQ in Manchester.

The clothing brand was the ITV2 dating show's official fashion partner this series, and Callum and Molly spent the day filming with them alongside Mike and Priscilla.

Callum showed up to the event wearing a loud patterned shirt and a pair of distressed skinny jeans, with he and Molly looking picture-perfect as she showed off her enviable figure in a fitted tan dress and white boots.

The 25-year-old model couldn't stop laughing when Callum changed into something a little less comfortable, teasing a glimpse at his chest tattoo in a ribbed dress.

The form-hugging outfit also hugged Callum's impressive physique - with his abs among the body parts proudly on display.
