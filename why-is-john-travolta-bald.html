Why is John Travolta bald?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 29, 2024

FANS of John Travolta were used to seeing him on screen with different hairstyles.

However, he has decided to sport a bald look in recent years.

Why is John Travolta bald?

On January 6, 2019, John Travolta debuted a bald look in a selfie he took with his daughter Ella, which was shared on his Instagram page.

He told People in an interview that: “It feels great. Listen, my new look came on a whim. It was a New Year's Eve idea. Because everyone liked it so much, I decided to keep it for a while. So, we’ll see.”

It is unclear why John decided to keep his hair bald.

However, he once shaved his head to play the role of a secret agent in the movie, From Paris With Love.

Also, based on a picture he posted with musician, Pitbull, on his Instagram page, fans think John Travolta was motivated by Pitbull to keep a bald look.

Who is John Travolta?

John Travolta was born on February 18, 1954, in Englewood, New Jersey.

John Travolta is a veteran actor, director, and producer who first came into the spotlight for his role in the television sitcom, Welcome Back, Kotter.

He also starred in Saturday Night Fever, Carrie, and Urban Cowboy.

He has been featured in several films since then.

He has also won several awards during his stellar career, such as the Golden Globe Award for Best Actor in a Motion Picture - Musical or Comedy.

He also received the IIFA Award for Outstanding Achievement in International Cinema in 2014.

In addition to his awards and accolades, he managed to earn a comfortable lifestyle from his career.

As of February 2023, John Travolta has an estimated net worth of about $250million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

His main source of wealth is said to be his career in the movie industry as an actor and director.

Is John Travolta married?

John Travolta's last marriage ended in 2020.

John tied the knot with actress Kelly Preston in 1991, however, she passed away on July 12, 2020.

She passed at the age of 57 at her home in Florida, after she had been diagnosed with breast cancer two years earlier.
