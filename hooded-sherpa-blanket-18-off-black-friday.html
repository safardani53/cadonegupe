Save 18 off this Hooded sherpa blanket ahead of Black Friday

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, April 29, 2024

WINTER is here, which means cosy clothes, nights on the sofa and Christmas films - and this hooded sherpa blanket is about to upgrade your night in.

I Want One Of Those (IWOOT), has created a Super Soft Sherpa Hoodie Fleece Blanket, and currently there's a handy £18 off.

  • Super Soft Sherpa Hoodie Fleece Blanket, £24.99 (Was £42.99) from IWOOT - buy here

IWOOT's Super Soft Sherpa Hoodie Fleece Blanket is on sale for almost half price, reduced from £42.99 to £24.99 - that's a handy saving of £18.

The Ultra Soft Hooded blanket is perfect for cold days and nights at home, as it is not only an insulating blanket, which doubles up as a wearable hoodie too.

And it's a great time to buy one, ahead of Black Friday.

It's is made from soft touch fabric, with a luxe Sherpa reverse side and features elasticated cuffs to ensure you are protected from the elements so you stay warm at all hours.

There's also a hood and double opening front pocket, so you can keep your hands snug, or store your valuables, so you really have no need to move when you're cosied up on the weekend.

It's perfect for working from home, especially if you're trying to cut down on energy costs this month and avoid turning on your heating - and we don't blame you.

It's one size fits all, and is suitable for adults and children, so you could even buy one for every member of the family this winter.

The hooded fleece is machine washable at 30 degrees, and is available in soft grey, blush pink and black.

The trio of colourways have all been reduced, but the price varies depending on the colour.

Prices start at £24.99 for the grey, and £29.99 (down from £52.99) down from pink.

IWOOT haven't said how long this deal will be around for, so we'd advise you shop quickly to bag the best deal.
