Ebay launches Wedding Hub with up to 2000 off designer dresses including Gucci and Suzanne Neville

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, April 27, 2024

IT'S nearly wedding season, and if you're getting ready for your big day, the top of the list for many is the dress.

With designer wedding dresses easily reaching the thousands, many brides are considering turning to second-hand retailers such as eBay.

And to make things even easier for soon-to-be brides, eBay has announced their brand new, pre-loved Wedding Hub.

Lovers can save up to £2000 off the RRP of many pre-loved bridal, bridesmaid and wedding guest dresses, suits and accessories.

The hub features sought-after designer brands including Suzanne Neville, Gucci and much more - we know, we can't believe it either.

READ MORE ABOUT WEDDINGS

With a wedding dress frequently cited as one of the biggest costs when getting married, eBay's preloved Wedding Hub is a great way to keep costs down.

More and more couples are looking to go pre-loved, with three in four (74%) people saying they would consider their “something old” as being a pre-loved wedding outfit, and 58% of "nearlyweds" looking to be more sustainable.

eBay has seen searches increase for pre-loved wedding dresses increase, with Vera Wang wedding dresses up by 33%, Suzanne Neville wedding dresses up by 113%, and Vivienne Westwood wedding dresses up by 28%.

But it's not just dresses, shoppers can also find great savings on a broad range of bridesmaid and guest outfits and accessories, from Ted Baker, Whistles, Reiss, and Ghost to name a few.

Kirsty Keoghan, Global GM of Fashion at eBay said: “As we approach peak wedding season, we hope our new wedding hub gives brides-to-be the option to get their dream wedding outfit, for a fraction of the price.

"We’re seeing more modern brides looking for wedding outfits that reflect their personality, and by choosing pre-loved, you really can find those unique options, while increasing the lifespan of these amazing dresses - which are often only worn once.

"With such a broad range of designer options available, there really is an outfit for everyone to dazzle in on their big day and options to suit all budgets, without compromising on style.”

Recent newlyweds Pip and Daniel Jolley revealed just how much money they saved by finding their wedding outfits on eBay: “Both being into vintage fashion from an early age, shopping for our big day on eBay was a no-brainer. 

"You only wear your wedding dress once, so it’s a waste of money buying new when you can find stunning styles for less. I found a show-stopping backless dress, and my husband found a 90s vintage Versace suit - for just £100 each!”
