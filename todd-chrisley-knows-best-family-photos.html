Photos Of The Chrisley Knows Best Stars Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, September 6, 2024

Todd and Julie Chrisley are in prison for tax evasion. See photos of the reality stars here.

Chrisley Knows Best parents Todd and Julie may be wealthy real estate moguls, but they’re also convicted tax evaders now. The family first found public attention on the reality television show Chrisley Knows Best, which premiered in the USA in 2014.

The big Chrisley Knows Best family includes Todd, Julie and their three children Savannah, Chase and their younger son Grayson. Nanny Faye Chrisley, Todd’s mother, is also a part of the family cast, which also includes Todd’s granddaughter Chloe Chrisley. Daughter Lindsie is estranged from the family.

Savannah and Chase, who are both in their 20s, have moved from Tennessee to Los Angeles and star in their own spin-off show as well, called Growing Up Chrisley. Todd and Julie were convicted on tax evasion and bank fraud charges in Jun. 2022. They both entered prison on Jan. 17, 2023. Julie will serve 7 years while Todd was sentenced to 12.
