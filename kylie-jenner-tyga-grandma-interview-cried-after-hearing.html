Kylie Jenner On Tyga’s Grandma’s Interview: She Cried After Hearing Loving Words

There is nothing quite like getting the seal of approval from your boyfriend’s family. Just ask Kylie!

“Kylie was moved to tears after hearing Tyga’s grandmother’s sweet words about her,” a source tells HollywoodLife.com EXCLUSIVELY. “It truly made her day to know that she’s made a great impression on the family of the man that she loves. She couldn’t feel more whole and complete.”

Among Kim’s most touching words for the luscious-lipped beauty? “We all love Kylie and think she is so much better for Tyga than Blac [Chyna],” she said. Wow, how good must that feel for our girl, Kylizzle?!

“She loves his grandmother, too. Kylie thinks she’s the cutest little lady ever!” adds the source. “She’s full of fire and spunk and Kylie loves that about her” Um, ditto, Ky-Ky!

“Kylie feels like she’s part of Tyga’s family now. She’s got two families that love her, that care about her and only want nothing but the best for her and Tyga. Kylie’s young, but she knows that family counts!” explains our insider. “She comes from a strong, loving and united family who, for the most part, stick together.”

Tyga’s Grandma Slams Blac Chyna: She Wasn’t ‘Nice,’ We’re Glad They Split

In the process of professing her love for Kylie, Grandma Kim threw major shade at Tyga’s ex and baby mama, Blac!

Kim, who has witnessed Blac partake in social media wars and petty cat fights, bluntly put her on blast, telling The Daily Mail, “Yes she is King’s mum, but Blac just wasn’t a very nice person.” Ouch!

But the dissing didn’t stop there. “We didn’t mind him splitting up from Blac,” Kim declared. “It was a relief for us all.” Dang, granny — tell us how you really feel!
