KELLEY'S HISTORY

Kelley was first introduced during season 24 of The Bachelor, which aired in January 2020.

Peter Weber admittedly had trouble choosing between the women on his season, having dated Hannah Ann Sluss and Madison Prewett along with Kelley. 

Following his splits with Hannah Ann and Madi, the pilot began to date the former attorney in April 2020. 

The pair spent much of lockdown during the pandemic together at her Chicago apartment and even made plans to move to New York City together. 

But they ended up breaking up in December 2020 - with Peter “blindsiding” Kelley with an announcement of their split on New Year’s Eve. 

However, they ended up getting back together in January and tried to “work on things” but ended things for good the following month in February 2021. 

Kelley claimed that she “found out something she wasn’t happy with” regarding Peter and teased that their relationship “ended really badly.”

PETER'S PATH

Meanwhile, Peter will not return to The Bachelor and is now “focused on turning his new children’s book into a TV show,” he exclusively told The Sun in November. 

The author of The Adventures Of Pilot Pete revealed that making a comeback to the ABC series that made him a household name is not in his playbook as he tries to fulfill his dreams of turning his latest project into a show.

In an exclusive interview with The Sun, Peter, 30, opened up about writing a very different book than most stars from Bachelor Nation have typically released. 

Referencing the many tell-all style tomes, including his ex Hannah Brown’s shocking book, the aviation expert said: “I could have done a tell-all book but I wasn’t interested in any of that. 

“I wanted to do something that would give back and give me an opportunity to do more of something I want to do. 

“My hope for this book is that it has longevity and inspired children from the day they read my book to decide ‘I want to do this and I want to do that.’ 

“This book was so easy for me to write because it was such an enjoyable process. It literally is my story from when I fell in love with flying as a little kid.”

He then discussed how this novel is just step one with the big plans he has, as Peter "has dreams of it to turn into a series hopefully one day.”

When asked if he will return to the Bachelor franchise, he said, “Oh, I don’t see myself going back, I’m more interested in making my own show.

“And we’ll see where this takes off, but I think it will, so that is where I will be putting my focus and attention to.” 

As for what he envisions for his show, the Los Angeles native said he would love to do an animated series where he, of course, is the voice of the title character, Pilot Pete.

However, the dream is in the "very early stages" still and nothing had officially been greenlit.

Last year, the former Delta pilot achieved another dream of his by being hired by United Airlines, where he had wanted to work since his dad flew planes for the same company during his childhood. 

Accomplishing that goal was such a big deal, it even caused the ending of his new book to be changed completely.
