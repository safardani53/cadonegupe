DWTS Star Cheryl Burke Turning Famous Wealthy Boyfriend Into Husband!

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, February 27, 2024

American dancer, model, and television host Cheryl Burke announced her engagement with boyfriend and they have been making plans for their wedding date and destination.

The California native is best known for her appearance on ABC's reality show, Dancing with the Stars (DWTS). She became the first female contestant to win the show and also the first to win twice and consecutively in 2006. She also appeared in the ending season of Lifetime's reality series, Dance Moms in 2017, replacing Abby Lee Miller, the principal cast member of the show.

Cheryl Burke's Bio; Parents, Sister, Ethnicity

The DWTS star was born on 3rd May 1984 to parents Sherri Charito and Stephen Burke. She belongs to a mixed ethnicity as her father is Irish-Russian and mother is of Philipino descent.

Her father, Stephen died on 9th March 2018, and her mother lives with her stepfather. She got reunited with her long-lost stepsister, Ina Burke after the death of her father in July 2018.

She even posted an Instagram picture with her sister resembling her excitement of meeting her which shed light to her darkness after the father's death.

Cheryl has a perfect body measurement with Breasts-Waist-Hips ratio 35-27-36 inches (89-68.5-91.5 cm). Standing tall at the towering height of 5 feet 4 inches (1.63 meters) her body weight is 58 kg (128 pounds).

Defending the weight loss, she explained that she had to change her lifestyle. She added that she is happy, eating well and working out every day being more responsible.

Cheryl Gained Popularity From Appearance In DWTS, Net Worth

At the early age of four, Cheryl began training in ballet, and at the age of 11, she started ballroom dancing. She first appeared on the second season of DWTS with singer and actor dancing partner Drew Lachey. The show gave the fame as she became the winner of the show.

On the third season, she won her second championship with a retired footballer, Emmitt Smith. She participated in 19 seasons, and her last appearance on DWTS was on the 25th season in 2016.

Apart from the DWTS, she participated in the first season of NBC's entertainment series; I Can Do That, where she finished second, losing to Nicole Scherzinger.

Cheryl Is Dating Joey's Brother, Matthew Lawrence
