Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 27, 2024

A BODY positive woman has candidly shared her exact measurements.

The five-foot-five, 150 pound influencer's honest post inspired other women to have a "healthier relationship" with their own figures.

Logan Hill (@loganraehill) posted a video of herself on TikTok in skin-tight green biker shorts and a matching crop top.

"Height: five-foot-five, weight: 155 to 160, jeans: 28, dress: six [or] eight, tops: medium, bra: 32 or 34D," she revealed.

The blonde beauty also shared some advice for women and their bodies.

"Also, people carry their weight differently," she said. "We are all unique and beautiful."

Logan's video was a response to a fan comment received on a previous post.

"You're amazing," the comment read. "I have a similar body type and I'm slowly starting to have a healthier relationship with my body because of you."

The TikTok drew in many viewers who shared their thoughts in the comment section.

"Omg we’re the same in everything and you look amazing this is a great day," one wrote.
