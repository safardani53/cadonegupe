Amir Khan treats himself to stunning 435k Lamborghini Urus Mansory.. but it's just a snip of his K

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, May 14, 2024

AMIR KHAN revealed he has treated himself to a stunning £435,000 Lamborghini Urus Mansory.

But it cost the boxing star just a snip of his Kell Brook fight purse.

The rivals settled their differences inside the ring last month following a 10-year war of words.

Thousands packed Manchester’s AO Arena as Brook KO’d Khan with a stunning sixth-round stoppage.

But although the winner walked off with a £3million purse, defeated Khan bagged an incredible £5m.

And he has now spent some of that dosh on a luxury new motor.

Khan, 35, is a huge petrolhead.

He boasts a wide range of high-end vehicles at home, including a £271k Lamborghini Aventador and a £233k Rolls-Royce Phantom.

But his latest purchase tops the lot, with Khan’s new Lamborghini Urus Mansory costing a whopping £435k.

Khan uploaded a picture to Instagram posing against the black-and-orange cruiser, with a caption saying: “Just bought myself a little gift.”

The SUV-style super high-end speedster has an incredible horsepower of 799.

It can reach a top speed of 205mph.

And the Urus can go from 0-60mph in only 3.3 seconds.

It is surely a worthy addition to Khan’s amazing collection, which also includes a £160k Porsche 911 Turbo and a £65k Range Rover.

And Khan still has over £4.5m of his fight purse left to splash out elsewhere.

REMATCH?

Meanwhile, the chances of a Brook-Khan rematch is still on the cards with Khan said to be mulling over the decision to activate the rematch clause..

Promoter Ben Shalom said: "I think the word on the street is true, Amir Khan doesn't want to call it a day.

"He's had a lot of time to think about the fight over the last few weeks, and he feels like he only had ten weeks to prepare and Kell Brook had six months.

"He feels hard done by."
