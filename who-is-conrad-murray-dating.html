Conrad Murray was born on a Thursday, February 19, 1953 in Grenada. His birth name is Conrad Robert Murray and he is currently 70 years old. People born on February 19 fall under the zodiac sign of Pisces. His zodiac animal is Serpent.

The trial of Conrad Murray was the American criminal trial of Michael Jackson’s personal physician, Conrad Murray. Murray was charged with involuntary manslaughter for causing Jackson’s death on June 25, 2009, from a massive overdose of the general anesthetic propofol. The trial, which started on September 27, 2011, was held in the Los Angeles County Superior Court in Los Angeles, California, before Judge Michael Pastor.

After moving to the United States to go to school, he graduated Texas Southern University in 1983 before going to Meharry Medical College. He was hired to be Jackson’s personal physician only a month before Jackson’s death.
