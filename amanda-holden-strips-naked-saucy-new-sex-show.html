Amanda Holden strips totally naked for saucy new TV show about sex

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, June 17, 2024

AMANDA Holden is set to bare all as she strips totally naked for her saucy new TV show Sex: A Bonkers History.

The Britain's Got Talent judge, 52, will ditch her silky robe and jump in a bathtub for the new five-part series.

Amanda will help uncover the saucy secrets of the last 2,500 years with historian Dan Jones in the Sky History series which will begin in September.

Ahead of its premier next month, The Sun can exclusively share a first-look clip of the trailer.

The series will use archive footage and dramatic reconstruction, with Amanda and Dan taking the leading roles to shed light on history’s best sex stories.

TV favourite Amanda will immerse herself by stripping off to enjoy a candlelit bath, while sporting Cleopatra's famous raven-coloured block of braids.

More on Amanda Holden

The hosts will explore how attitudes towards sex and sexuality have evolved throughout the ages, from the origination of the first contraceptive in ancient Egypt to swinging in 70s suburban Britain.

The pair take a look at how historical sexualisation of Ancient Egypt's Cleopatra ultimately overshadowed her true achievements as a strong, calculated, and intelligent ruler.

Enjoying a soak from her bathtub, Amanda says: "History on the whole has been written by men.

"So sorry Cleopatra, despite all your accomplishments, your savvy political manoeuvring and incredible intellect, you'll forever be remembered as the woman who bonked her way to the top."

Along the way, the pair will learn how Tudor sexual cravings altered the path of British history, and they'll dig into Georgian Britain to discover that its prim and proper façade couldn't be more deceptive.

The pair will also reveal how the Victorian era was secretly one of the most sexually progressive in the world.

Lastly, they'll discover that sexual attitudes in the 20th century have changed more than in any other period in history and that this
rate of change only continues to accelerate.

The Heart radio star will dress up as four more iconic and powerful women throughout history - from the Tudor period as Queen Elizabeth I, through the Georgian and Victoria era as Queen Charlotte and Queen Victoria.

Amanda will also channel her inner Marilyn Munroe as the series explores historical women from the 20th century.

According to the synopsis, viewers can "expect saucy surprises" and "forget what you thought you knew", as Amanda and Dan will throw themselves into immersive history and bring us some unique and intriguing insights in Sex: A Bonkers History.

Speaking about her involvement in the new show, Amanda said: “Believe me when I say our generation knows nothing about sex compared to the debauched antics of our ancestors.

“My goodness, I’ve really had my eyes opened, and had a real laugh, during the filming of this brilliant new series.

“I have left it to Dan Jones to get his hands dirty whilst I’ve been on the sidelines as a very entertained voyeur.”

Read more on The Sun

Owen Gay, Creative Director at One Tribe TV said: “One Tribe TV is excited to be working with Amanda Holden and Dan Jones, two people who have the sensitivity, knowledge, and fun to make this series the history extravaganza it should be as we tackle the universal but often taboo subject of sex through the ages."

Sex: A Bonkers History premieres on Sky HISTORY in September 2023.
