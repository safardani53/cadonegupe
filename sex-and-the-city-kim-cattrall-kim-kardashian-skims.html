Sex and the City star Kim Cattrall, 67, wows in nude lingerie from Kim Kardashian's Skims brand

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, July 7, 2024

ACTRESS Kim Cattrall becomes Skims Cattrall as she poses in the brand’s underwear.

Kim, 67, modelled nude lingerie from Skims, reality star Kim Kardashian’s shapewear brand.

She said she felt “freaking fabulous” in a video posted on Instagram.

Kim returned for a cameo in the series two finale of Sex and the City spin-off And Just Like That.

She had not played sex siren Samantha Jones since 2010 because of a feud with Carrie actress Sarah Jessica Parker.

Back in 2018, Sarah told Watch What Happens Live host Andy Cohen that she was "heartbroken" after Kim said she was never friends with her co-stars.

Then in 2019, Kim shut down any possibility of her reprising the role of Samantha ever again.

During an interview with The Observer in 2019, she said: "I went past the finish line playing Samantha Jones because I loved Sex And The City.

"It was a blessing in so many ways but after the second movie, I’d had enough.

"I couldn’t understand why they wouldn’t just replace me with another actress instead of wasting time bullying. No means no.”
