Kardashian fans beg Kim to date her ex who was 'the one that got away' - and Kanye West won't like i

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, February 11, 2024

KIM Kardashian's fans have begged the star to date her ex Reggie Bush following her split from Pete Davidson.

The reality star, 41, was in an on-and-off relationship with the NFL star, 37, from 2007 to 2010.

Reggie has since moved on with his wife, Armenian dancer Lilit, 34, and has three kids with her.

But that hasn't stopped Kim's fans from pining for their romance on Reddit.

One fan shared photos of the exes from their relationship on Reddit and wrote: "The one that got away.

"I will forever ship Kim with Reggie, they were the most attractive couple ever (can’t believe she went from this to Pete). What a plot twist it would be if they ever got back together, but Kim is a totally different person now."

Another added: "Always got the impression that Reggie was her ideal type. Like when she thinks of her dream man, it’s Reggie, at least in the physical sense."

A third user posted: "The way she looks at him with these dreamy eyes!"

Kim and Reggie began dating in 2007 after they were introduced by a mutual friend at an awards show.

They split in 2010 and Reggie went on to date country singer Jesse James in 2010, before meeting his future wife Lilit in 2011.

Meanwhile, Kim began dating Kanye West in 2011 and they had four kids together - North, nine, Saint, six, Chicago, four, and Psalm, three.

The SKIMS founder filed for divorce in February 2021.

She moved on with Pete Davidson in November 2021 but they split last month.

The Kardashians star and former SNL funnyman have broken up, and decided to just be friends, E! News reported.

A source said: "They have a lot of love and respect for each other but found that the long distance and their demanding schedules made it really difficult to maintain a relationship."

An insider later claimed Kim broke up with Pete because she was "totally exhausted" by his "immaturity" and their 13-year age gap.

Pete has been spending part of the summer in Australia working on his new movie, David Michôd's comedy, Wizards!

Aside from Reggie, Kim's fans have been predicting for months that she will date Van Jones.
