What is Julia Haart’s net worth?

Trend net worth have said she is “one of America’s top 10 fashion designers” and that she is sought after by many celebrities for collaborations, putting $3.5m as her current net worth.

She gained this hefty net worth as a self-made woman. In 2013 she founded her own shoe company, branded ‘Julia Haart’. Three years later, she collaborated with La Perla for their 2016 Spring and fall accessory collections and later became creative director for them.
