Harry Kane spends long-awaited Christmas break on beach after vowing to wind up England pals over fe

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, March 13, 2024

HARRY KANE looked set to live up to his promise to wind-up his England pals as he soaked-up the sun on his Christmas getaway.

The Three Lions captain, 30, left boyhood club Tottenham in the summer to sign for Bayern Munich in a £104m deal.

The striker has more than lived up to his price tag since, scoring 25 times so far for the German giants.

Kane recently revealed the difficulties he and his loved ones had settling into a new country and learning the language.

He had been living in a whopping £10,000-a-night suite at the Vier Jahreszeiten Kempinski five-star hotel but has now finally found a home for his wife and kids.

And now after settling into their new surroundings, England and Spurs’ all-time top scorer has whisked his family away for Christmas.

His wife, childhood sweetheart Katie Goodland, shared a snap on social media of her man standing on some golden sand on a beach while looking out into the crystal blue ocean as their children played next to him.

That was captioned with a love-heart emoji and the words: “My loves.”

And that came with another shot of one of their daughters decked out in red and white along with reindeer antlers on her head and the words: “Boxing day vibes.”

The couple have four children together, Ivy, Vivienne Jane, Louis and new baby boy Henry, who was the latest addition to the family when he was born on August 20, 2023.

Kane is used to providing plenty of festive fun to Spurs supporters after scoring an impressive 22 goals in matches taking place between December 26 and January 4 for the club.

But while his old team have a trip to Brighton tomorrow followed by a home clash with Bournemouth on New Year’s Eve, he is taking full advantage of the Bundesliga’s winter break.

He recently revealed: “We're going somewhere hot, so we'll spend 10 days and just enjoy that time.

“I'm going to text all my mates in England a picture of me on the beach somewhere.”

Now he has done just that, courtesy of Katie, although he has still been on our screens in the lead-up to Crimbo.

Kane starred in a Christmas-themed Amazon advert as he tried to earn his Spurs only to fail while whipping up a Christmas dinner.

At one point in the ad he was forced to pull out a fire extinguisher in order to to put out some burned roast potatoes before he was later seen staring at a serving tray as if it is the Bundesliga trophy.

And in a possible nod to teasing his England pals, he was spotted chatting online to new Spurs hero James Maddison, who will unfortunately also have Christmas off due to an injury.
