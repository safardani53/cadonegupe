Marvin Minoff is an American television and film producer best known for producing The Nixon Interviews by British journalist David Frost for former US President Richard Nixon in 1977. Minoff also co-produced with business partner Mike Farrell and others, the 1998 film Patch Adams, starring Robin Williams.
