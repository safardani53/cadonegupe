Falynn Pina and Jaylan Banks Recently Welcomed Their First Child

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Months ago, 'RHOA' star Falynn Pina Guobadia revealed she was pregnant with Jaylan Banks's child. How many kids does she have?

With the bombshell revelation of former Real Housewives of Atlanta star Porsha Williams' engagement to Simon Guobadia, Simon’s ex-wife, Falynn Pina, has finally spoken her piece about the drama. Although there were disgraceful accusations thrown out against her, Falynn has since cleared her name and has been focused on moving on with her life. And that includes keeping her family as a No.1 priority.

While the world has been vocal with their opinions about Falynn moving on with her best friend, Jaylan Banks Pina, she continues to live life unapologetically on her own terms. After revealing in August 2021 that she was expecting a child with her new beau, the star recently shared that she's given birth.

Now, fans have a few questions about her family life. For starters, how many kids does Falynn have? Here’s what we know.

Falynn and Jaylan have officially welcomed daughter Emma.

Oh baby! On Nov. 27, 2021, Falynn broke social media by announcing the arrival of her baby girl, Emma Sang Pina. In an adorable Instagram post that shows Baby Emma's teeny-tiny fist, the reality star shared the great news with fans.

"Emma Sang Pina," Falynn wrote with a pink bow emoji. "She’s absolutely perfect and healthy. 11.26.21"

Fans immediately flocked to Falynn's Instagram page to congratulate her.

"So happy for you guys. Such a blessing," one fan commented.

"Finally a girl. She’s going to be so loved and spoiled," another fan commented.

Since the announcement, fans have been waiting with bated breath for images of Baby Emma to surface. However, in a YouTube interview with blogger Tisa Tells, Falynn explains that she's in no rush to show Emma off to the world since social media users have been falling prey to rumors spread by Falynn's ex-husband, Simon Guobadia.

Now, with the arrival of Baby Emma, Falynn is officially a mother to four children. She has three sons from previous relationships: 14-year-old Troy, 13-year-old Dylan, and 8-year-old Liam.

Back in August 2021, Falynn and Jaylan revealed, via their YouTube channel, that they were expecting a baby. The pair also shared that they formally changed their last name to Pina, as a way to honor Falynn’s grandfather.

In October 2021, Falynn and Jaylan Banks Pina announced their engagement.

On Oct. 17, 2021, Falynn took to Instagram to share that she and Jaylan are officially engaged to be married.

“I said YES!!! I have no clue how #itsjaylanbanks pulled this big surprise off but I have not stopped balling my eyes out. I love you baby #PerfectlyPina,” Falynn wrote with crying-face and heart emojis.

Fans immediately congratulated the couple on taking the next step in their relationship.

“Beautiful ... Congratulations hun. You deserve it. You are so strong, courageous, and selfless! Many blessings to you,” one commented.

“I’m so happy for your next chapter with your fiancé, the boys, and the baby girl … Congratulations to the Pinas,” another fan wrote.

In July 2021 — after months of social media speculation — Jaylan revealed that he and Falynn had decided to take their relationship from platonic to romantic. In an Instagram post, he shared a photo of them both with a sentimental caption.

“A man is truly a man when he earns the respect of a good woman and keeps her trust,” Jaylan wrote.

While the couple have received tons of support, naysayers have also shared their opinions about their union. But despite some of the negative chatter, both Falynn and Jaylan have remained focused on their family and personal business ventures.

Even though Falynn has been through the unimaginable with her ex-husband, it’s nice to see the star get her happy ending. Congratulations to Falynn and Jaylan on their adorable baby and upcoming nuptials!
