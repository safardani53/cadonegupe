Beauty fans racing to Home Bargains for Glow Recipe toner dupe thats 30 cheaper &amp; you get more

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, March 9, 2024

BARGAIN-HUNTING beauty fans are raving about an ''amazing'' dupe for the cult favourite Glow Recipe toner that's selling for less than £2.

Home Bargains have once again blown away money-saving shoppers with a new skincare product that is believed to be a ''dupe'' for a big brand - but it's £30 cheaper.

The discount store has long been a favourite for many and now fans have raced to TikTok to rave about a toner they reckon is a replica for the Watermelon Glow PHA + BHA Pore-Tight Toner.

The budget-friendly version, called the So Fresh Watermelon Watermelon Crush Pore-Tight Toner, costs £1.25 for a 200ml bottle.

Meanwhile, the posh version would set beauty fans back by an eye-watering £31 for a 150ml bottle from Cult Beauty.

Not only are you saving a whopping £29.75, but you're also getting 50ml extra - cha-ching.

Similarly to the real deal, the Home Bargains' version, too, is infused with watermelon extract, which will soothe and calm your skin, whilst also minimising the appearance of pores.

Both toners also feature Hyaluronic Acid which is known for delivering long-lasting hydration to skin, especially when it's feeling a little dry and dull.

One social media beauty influencer, only known as @x.1ifestyles, also recently shared the cheap product her followers, adding in the caption "Home Bargains slayed."

Another fan responded to the clip, writing: "i have it and it’s so good it’s £1.25."

A fellow shopper aso raved about the bargain buy, writing: ''I just got this not even 20 mins ago and it’s so good.''

"I got it it's so nice omg IT SMELLS AMAZING,'' a third chimed in.

Whilst shopping at the store, don't forget to check out their perfume collection.

Recently, fans spotted a £1.99 scent they said was an exact dupe for £235 a bottle, Baccarat Rouge.

One person said: “I bought this, couldn’t think what it smelt of … but it’s very strong, great bargain.”

Meanwhile, one perfume fan noted: "Omg my fav!"
