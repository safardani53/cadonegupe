4 Things We Learned From Rachelle Lefevre on The Talk: Trouble on the Horizon

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, March 4, 2024

Rachelle Lefevre joined the ladies of The Talk on August 1st to discuss the second season of Under The Dome. Here's what we learned:

1. Rachelle thought she might be getting killed off at the end of Season 1.

2. Executive Producer Neal Baer gives the actors 2-3 episodes notice before their deaths.

3. The Junior-Big Jim relationship will get creepier and more intense.

4. There's trouble on the horizon for Julia and Barbie.
