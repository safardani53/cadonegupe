[VIDEO] Leah Messers Wedding Marries Jeremy Calvert On Teen Mom 2 Hollywood Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, May 21, 2024

Leah Messer & Jeremy Calvert Tie The Knot

Leah and Jeremy had gotten married weeks before on April 4, 2012 at Catlettsburg Wedding Chapel in Catlettsburg, Ky. so that they could apply for a home loan together. But on their big special day, they pull out all the stops for their rainy South Carolina lakeside wedding.

Leah arrives in a white stretch limo dressed in a gorgeous white gown, and had her hair down in long, flowy curls. Jeremy keeps his look more casual in a loose white short-sleeved button-up — perfect for a beach wedding!

Leah’s Dad & Stepdad Walk Her Down The Aisle

On the April 8 episode, Leah called her biological father to see if he would be interested in walking her down the aisle for her big day. And it looks like her dad Gary Messer comes through, because she walks down the aisle arm-in-arm with both Gary and her stepdad Lee Spears.

Her twins Ali and Aleeah Simms from her previous marriage to Corey Simms were also there dressed in white, and totally stole the show as flower girls.
