Jenelle Evans Reflects on &quot;Bad Boyfriends &amp; Rough Relationships&quot; For MTV Special

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, June 23, 2024

Back in September, Jenelle Evans married David Eason in a lovely ceremony with a suitably dramatic build-up.

Evans nearly called off the wedding just hours before saying, “I do,” but following their last blow-out fight as an unmarried couple, she and Eason eventually went through with it.

The whole incident served as a reminder that this is no fairytale ending for Jenelle, but rather the beginning of a new chapter in her life that’s sure to bring its own challenges and difficulties.

It was also a reminder of how many unhealthy and tempestuous relationships Jenelle endured prior to meeting the right guy.

Sure, the disasters in Jenelle’s love life were often self-created, but there’s no denying that she wound up with some real pieces of work over the years.

The Teen Mom landscape is littered with ex-cons, addicts, and abusers, but surely no one has wound up with quite as many cretins as Ms. Evans.

And now, they’re all back to tell their tale in an MTV special that’s focusing on the men who have come and gone from Jenelle’s life.

Teen Mom 2: Jenelle, The Ex Files is set to air tonight, and even the most diehard fans might have forgotten about some of the esoteric exes who are about to crawl back into the spotlight.

Remember Jenelle’s first husband, Courtland Rogers?

Courland just got out of jail, which likely doesn’t come as much of a surprise if you witnessed the lawless lifestyle he and Jenelle led.

How about Jenelle’s ex-fiance Gary Head?

Fun fact: He’s one of five guys Evans was engaged to at one point (she only walked down the aisle with two of them).

And then there’s Jace’s dad, the seldom-seen Andrew Lewis, who will reportedly appear on tonight’s show.

And who could forget Nathan Griffith, with whom Jenelle is still locked in a legal battle over custody of their son, Kaiser?

As the Ashley’s Reality Roundup reports, MTV somehow got Jenelle to narrate this thing, which should elevate the special to a new level of cringe-inducing awkwardness.

Obviously, we can’t wait.

Follow the link to watch Teen Mom 2 online and relive Jenelle’s angsty love life in time for tonight’s show.

Not only will you be entertained, you’re sure to instantly feel better about your own dating history!
